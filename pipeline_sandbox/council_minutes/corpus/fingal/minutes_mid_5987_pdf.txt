F/229/24
   (Agenda Item No )
ANNOUNCEMENTS
It was AGREED by the members that Deputy Mayor, Councillor G. Maguire chair 
the meeting. 
 
At the start of the meeting, the Deputy Mayor, Councillor. G. Maguire reminded 
Members that Standing Orders would apply to all aspects of the meeting.
The Deputy Mayor welcomed the invited visitors, Councillors and members of 
the public to the meeting.  
 
 
F/230/24
   (Agenda Item No 1)
Returning Officers Report of the 2024 Local Elections
Ms. Nikki Halleran, Meetings Administrator, read into the record of the meeting the 
return of persons elected to Fingal County Council on 7th June 2024.
 
F/231/24
   (Agenda Item No 2)
Election of Mayor and Deputy Mayor
The Deputy Mayor Councillor G. Maguire on behalf of the outgoing Mayor 
former Councillor A. Henchy congratulated each of the members on their 
election to Fingal County Council. 
 
The Deputy Mayor Councillor G. Maguire complimented former Councillor A. 
Henchy on his role as Mayor of Fingal and on the manner in which he conducted 
the role of Mayor of Fingal throughout the past year.
 
She then thanked the Elected Members, for the support they have given to the 
Mayor and Deputy Mayor over the last 12months. 
 
Several Members then complimented the outgoing Mayor and Deputy Mayor on 
the manner in which they conducted their roles throughout the past year.
 

3
The Chief Executive, welcomed the newly elected members.  The Chief Executive 
complimented the Mayor on his role as Mayor of Fingal and thanked him on her 
own behalf and on the behalf of the staff for his work throughout the year.  
F/232/24
   (Agenda Item No 2a)
Election of Mayor
The Deputy Mayor, Councillor G. Maguire then asked for nominations for the 
election of the Mayor.
 
It was proposed by Councillor M. McCamley, seconded by Councillor J. Walsh:
 
“That Councillor Brian McDonagh be elected Mayor.”
 
It was then proposed by Councillor P. Mulville, seconded by Councillor J. 
Burtchaell:
 
“That Councillor Dean Mulligan be elected Mayor.”
 
The Deputy Mayor, Councillor G. Maguire then instructed that a roll call vote be 
taken and on a division the voting resulted as follows:

F/233/24
   (Agenda Item No )
Address by the Mayor of Fingal, Councillor Brian McDonagh
The Mayor outlined how privileged he is to be Mayor of Fingal, to serve those living 
in Fingal, people who visit as tourists and particularly to people who are new to 
Fingal.  The Mayor congratulated everyone who was elected to serve the people of 
Fingal
 
The Mayor thanked his family, friends and colleagues for their support over the 
years.
