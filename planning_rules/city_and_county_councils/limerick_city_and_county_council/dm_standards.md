## Residential density
- Serviced sites: generally **10 housing units per hectare**.
- (Wider density guidance in Chapter 2 Core Strategy.)

## Plot ratio / site coverage
- Enterprise/employment (greenfield): **40–50%** site coverage.
- Urban-centre locations: plot ratio **1:5**; **70%** site coverage expected.

## Private open space (houses)

| House type | Minimum area |
|---|---|
| 1–2 bedroom | 48 m² |
| 3–5 bedroom | 60–75 m² |
| Inner urban / mews | 25 m² |

- Front garden: minimum **6 m** length.
- Rear garden depth: minimum **11 m** (giving 22 m back-to-back).

## Public open space
- Greenfield sites: **15%** multi-functional open space.
- Brownfield/infill: **10%** minimum.
- Play-lot provision (schemes >50 units): **4 m² per residential unit**.

Open space hierarchy: play lot/pocket park 400 m²–0.2 ha (100 m walk); small park 0.2–2 ha (400 m); local park 2–20 ha (1 km).

## Apartment floor areas (minimum)

| Unit type | Minimum area |
|---|---|
| Studio | 37 m² |
| 1-bed | 45 m² |
| 2-bed (3 person) | 63 m² |
| 2-bed (4 person) | 73 m² |
| 3-bed | 90 m² |

- 10+ unit schemes: majority should exceed minimum by 10% (excl. studios).
- Minimum 50% dual-aspect units (discretion for refurb/infill <0.25 ha).
- Apartment storage: 1-bed 3 m²; 2-bed(3p) 5 m²; 2-bed(4p) 6 m²; 3-bed 9 m².

## Separation / privacy
- Apartments (up to 3 storeys): **22 m** between opposing windows.
- Houses: **22 m** between directly opposing rear first-floor windows; **3 m** side separation between dwellings.

## Rural one-off housing
- Minimum site area: **0.2 hectares (0.5 acres)**.
- Minimum road frontage: **30 m**.
- Gates recessed **4.5 m** behind hedgerow line; 45-degree splay to road.

## Sightline visibility distances
- Petrol stations: road speed <80 km/h — **160 m** minimum; ≥80 km/h — **215 m** minimum.
- Rural dwelling entrances: per TII road geometric standards by road category (specific x/y not quantified in chapter).
- Petrol-station pump island: ≥**7 m** from roadside boundary.

## EV charging
- Employment/enterprise: EV charging point(s) per unit (ratios in DM Table 8a/8b).
- Petrol stations: rapid EV charging recommended where café/sit-down facilities operate.

## Bin / refuse storage
- Houses: location and design shown in application.
- External storage in business parks: ≥**2 m** screening height.

## SuDS / drainage
- Attenuation designed for 1-in-100-year return period + **30%** climate-change allowance; urban creep allowance **10%**.
- Surface-water network: 5-year return period; minimum pipe diameter **DN225**; orifice >**50 mm**.

## Car / cycle parking
- Referenced to **DM Table 8a/8b** (Section 11.8.4); specific numeric ratios are in that table, which was not rendered in the captured text excerpt.

## Other
- Landscaping maintenance: minimum 18 months; tree establishment 5 years.
- Phasing schedule required for residential developments exceeding 30 units.
