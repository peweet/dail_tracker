# Required Assessments / Triggers — Westmeath County Council

**Plan:** Westmeath County Development Plan 2021-2027 — Volume 1 Written Statement
**Fetch date:** 2026-06-13 · Source: `raw/volume1_written_statement.pdf`

- **Appropriate Assessment (AA)** — the Habitats Directive requires the impacts of any plans/projects likely to affect **Natura 2000** sites to be assessed; all projects/plans arising are **screened** for the need to undertake AA. Plan-level AA was carried out.
- **Flood Risk Assessment** — a **Strategic Flood Risk Assessment (SFRA)** is required and informs zoning; site-specific FRA considers sources, flood-water flow paths and flood-risk indicators (Justification Test per the 2009 Guidelines).
- **Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)** — applied per national thresholds; references the **Environmental Impact Assessment Sub-Threshold Development Guidelines (2003)** for sub-threshold screening.
- **Architectural Heritage / Protected Structures** — protection of protected structures and conservation areas addressed in the built-heritage chapter; AHIA expectation for affecting works.
- **Traffic & Transport Assessment** — TTA required for larger developments per the plan's transport policies.

> Most AA/EIA/SFRA detail sits in the plan's environment and core-strategy chapters rather than Chapter 16; recorded here as referenced, without inference on thresholds.
