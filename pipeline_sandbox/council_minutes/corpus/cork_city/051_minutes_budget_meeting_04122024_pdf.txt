APOLOGIES: 
            Comhairleoir T. Shannon 
 
 
An tArdmhéara Comhairleoir D. Boyle recited the opening prayer and observed one minute’s reflection. 
An tArdmhéara outlined the proposed format for the meeting which included that Elected Members’ 
speeches would be limited to 3 minutes in duration.  He then thanked the Chief Executive, Chief Financial 
Officer and officials in Cork City Council for the work put into preparing the Draft 2025 Budget. 
 
 
 
 
 
 

 
 
1. 
REPORT FROM CHIEF EXECUTIVE 
 
The Chief Executive presented her Report on the 2025 Draft Budget for Cork City Council.  
A number of Members spoke on the issues and challenges associated with the Draft Budget 2025.

6. 
APPROVAL OF VACANCY ABATEMENT SCHEME 
 
6.1  
On the proposal of Comhairleoir O. Moran, seconded by Comhairleoir J. Maher, the following 
motion was proposed; 
 
‘That Cork City Council makes a scheme for the abatement of rates due to it by liable persons, or 
classes of persons, in respect of vacant properties in accordance with the provisions Section 9 of 
the Local Government Rates and Other Matters Act 2019, as amended. 
Such a scheme in respect of vacant property will provide for an abatement of 40% of the rates 
due to CorkCity Council by a liable person for the financial year ending 31 December 2025” 
 
A vote was taken where there appeared as follows: 
 
 
FOR:  
Comhairleoirí J. Maher, J. Kavanagh, M. McDonnell, O. Moran, T. Fitzgerald,

(23)
