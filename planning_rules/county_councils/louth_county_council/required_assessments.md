# Louth County Council — Required Assessments / Assessment Triggers

**Authority:** Louth County Council
**Plan:** Louth County Development Plan 2021-2027
**Chapter:** Chapter 13 — Development Management Guidelines
**Source URL:** https://www.louthcoco.ie/en/publications/development-plans/louth-county-development-plan-2021-2027/chapter-13-web-.pdf
**Fetch date:** 2026-06-13

> Triggers as stated in Chapter 13. No inference applied.

## Site Suitability / Wastewater (Rural housing)
- For one-off rural houses on individual on-site wastewater treatment systems: system must have capacity to accommodate additional loading per EPA Code of Practice: Domestic Waste Water Treatment Systems (p.e. 10). May require upgrade of existing systems.

## Transport / Workplace Travel Plan (13.16.13)
- Workplace Travel Plan / Mobility Management Plan required for developments generating significant trip demand — new schools or developments with in excess of 100 employees. Town-centre location, employment type and shift pattern may also trigger.
- Transport Mobility Management Plan required to support any reduction in car parking.

## School Demand Report (13.8.25)
- Any development in excess of 200 residential units must include a report identifying school-place demand generated.

## Design Statement (13.8.8)
- Required for any development in excess of 10 residential units (design principles, Urban Design Manual 12 criteria, accordance with plan objectives).

## Heritage / Birds & Habitats
- Chapter 13 references the Birds and Habitats Directives in the context of development assessment (Appropriate Assessment screening applies under the plan's environmental framework).

## Note
Chapter 13 is the Development Management Guidelines chapter; full enumerations of Appropriate Assessment, EIA/EIAR thresholds and Architectural Heritage Impact Assessment / ACA / protected structure procedures are distributed across the plan (Chapters 3, 9 heritage, and environmental volumes). The captured chapter explicitly states the operational triggers above. Treat AA/EIA/AHIA detail as partial.
