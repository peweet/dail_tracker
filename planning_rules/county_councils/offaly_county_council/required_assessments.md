# Required Assessments — Offaly County Council

**Authority:** Offaly County Council
**Plan:** Offaly County Development Plan 2021-2027
**Chapter:** Chapter 13 — Development Management Standards (section "Assessments Required for Certain Projects")
**Source URL:** https://www.offaly.ie/app/uploads/Council/Council_Services_A-Z/Planning_Building/Chapter-13-Development-Management-Standards.pdf
**Fetch date:** 2026-06-13

Triggers enumerated verbatim in the chapter:

## Appropriate Assessment (Natura 2000 / SAC / SPA)
- All plans or projects (including the Council's own Part 8 proposals), unless directly connected with/necessary to the management of a Natura 2000 site, "are required to be subject to screening for Appropriate Assessment."
- Full Appropriate Assessment (Natura Impact Statement) required unless screening establishes no likely significant effect on a Natura 2000 site.

## Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA / EIAR)
- Mandatory thresholds per Planning and Development Regulations 2001 (as amended).
- Sub-threshold EIAR required where the Planning Authority considers a sub-threshold proposal likely to have significant environmental effects.

## Flood Risk Assessment
- Regard to The Planning System and Flood Risk Management Guidelines (DEHLG/OPW 2009).
- "All development proposals within or incorporating areas at moderate to high risk of flooding will require site specific and appropriately detailed Flood Risk Assessments."
- "All development proposals within or incorporating areas at moderate or high risk of flooding will require the application of the Development Management Justification Test."
- Sequential approach to inform site layout; must demonstrate no increase in flood risk elsewhere.
- (DMS-106 Flood Risk Assessments restates these requirements.)

## Other Assessments (chapter lists, "not exhaustive")
- Traffic and Transport Assessment
- Landscape Character Assessment
- Visual Impact Assessment
- Archaeological Assessment
- Architectural Heritage Assessment
- Ecological Impact Assessment

## Traffic & Transport Assessment (DMS-105)
- Detailed Transport and Traffic Assessment (TTA) required where new developments have a significant effect on travel demand / capacity of surrounding transport links, per thresholds in Tables 2.1, 2.2, 2.3 of TII Traffic and Transport Assessment Guidelines (2014). Road Safety Audits may also be required.

## Architectural Heritage / Protected Structures
- DMS-93: Protected Structures (including development within view of / within the curtilage, attendant grounds and setting of Protected Structures).
- DMS-94: Architectural Heritage Assessment report.
- DMS-95: Architectural Conservation Areas (ACA).
- DMS-96: Archaeological Sites.

## Energy / Climate Statements
- Energy Efficiency and Climate Change Adaptation Design Statement: residential developments in excess of 10 units (DMS-31); retail/industrial/warehousing/business-park developments in excess of 1,000 m² (DMS-60, DMS-74).

## Site Suitability / Wastewater (rural)
- On-site wastewater treatment per EPA Code of Practice on Wastewater Treatment and Disposal Systems serving Single Houses (October 2009) — DMS-44, DMS-51 (implies site-suitability/percolation testing to EPA Code).
