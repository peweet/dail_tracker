At the outset, Mr. Uinsinn Finn, Director of Services, expressed appreciation to staff in the
Roads Department and the Area Offices who had been involved in bringing together the
Roads Programme and noted there was a 15% increase in the overall national grant income
for County Galway compared to a 0.7% increase nationally.
Mr. Harley noted there was a multitude of funding sources across the total programme
valued at 79m, including 46 million from the Department of Transport, 12 million from
the Council's own resources, 2.6 million from NTA Active Travel, 500,0o0 from the NTA

within the Loughrea Municipal District.

stated that the selection of roads for inclusion in the programme was based on overall needs

Mr. O'Loughlin, Mr. Duane and Mr. Gavin for the significant work undertaken in preparing
the Draft Roads Programme.
