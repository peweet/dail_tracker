5. Matters Arising. 
The LCDC paid tribute and the moment of silence to the former Lord Mayor of Cork 
Donal Counihan who has passed away. The Committee acknowledged Mr 
Counihan’s tremendous work and advocacy across the city. 
6. For Information. 
a. Cork City Council Community Development Grants 2026. 
Martin Davoren presented the LCDC updates regarding the Cork City Council 
Community Development Grants 2026 to the LCDC. He noted that applications 
are now finalised. The payments are going to be processed in due course. 
b. LCDC Loal Enhancement Programme Grants 2026. 
Martin Davoren updated the LCDC regarding the LCDC LEP Grants for 2026.  The 
returns documents are sent back to the department (DRCDG). The department 
will respond in due course. 
c. LCDC Communications Campaign – Ministerial Launch on 1st May 2026. 
Kieran O’Connell talked through the launch of the LCDC Communications 
campaign and the synopsis of the launch to the Committee. The ministerial 
launch will be held on 1st May 2026.  
d. German Embassy in Dublin request to meet LCDC members – Thursday, 
21st May 2026 at 15.00 p.m. in Cork City Hall. 
Kieran O’Connell presented to the LCDC a request from the German embassy, 
who have a delegation of 10 senior German politician who wish to sit with the 
LCDC to discuss community work and policy development the LCDC deliver on. 
All LCDC members are requested to attend this event and welcome and engage 
the delegation.  
Denis Leamy advised the LCDC to confirm their attendance to Kieran O’Connell.
