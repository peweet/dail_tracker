b 
Portmarnock Sports and Leisure Club (PSLC)  
 
The report by the Chief Executive which had been circulated was CONSIDERED. 
It was proposed by Councillor E. O’Brien and seconded by Councillor A. Tormey 
and RESOLVED that: 
“In recognition of the unique circumstances in which PSLC, a community 
organisation, has funded, provided and operated a major piece of recreational 
infrastructure on Council lands, and considering it necessary and desirable to 
promote the interests of the local community and the recreational and 
community development of the area, Fingal County Council, in accordance with 
Section 66 of the Local Government Act 2001 (as Amended), hereby resolves - 
       that a grant of €500,000 be awarded as a financial contribution towards 
the cost of essential upgrade works at Portmarnock Sports & Leisure Club, 
in order to support the expedited delivery and early completion of the 
urgent and necessary works and to facilitate the reopening of the 
swimming pool for public use, 
       that the provision of the grant is subject to satisfactory financial, technical 
and governance review, including appropriate scrutiny and verification of 
costs, funding arrangements and delivery proposals 
       and that the financial contribution shall be funded from funds provided 
for within the community fund available to non-Council owned community 
facilities.”
