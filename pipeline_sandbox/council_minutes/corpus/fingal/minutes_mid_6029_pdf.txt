   (Agenda Item No 20) 
Draft Flemington Local Area Plan 
 
The report by the Chief Executive which had been circulated was CONSIDERED.  
A presentation was given to the Members by Patricia Cadogan, A/Senior Planner, 
Planning & Strategic Infrastructure Department. 
  
During the course of the discussion, it was proposed by Councillor T. O’Leary and 
seconded by Councillor A. Tormey: 
  
“This Council resolves to delete Figure 9.1, ‘Phasing of Development’ from the 
Flemington Local Area Plan.” 
  
The report by the Chief Executive which had been circulated was CONSIDERED. 
Following discussion, the report was AGREED. 
  
It was then proposed by Councillor T. O’Leary and seconded by Councillor A. 
Tormey: 
  
“This Council resolves to delete Table 9.1 – ‘Phasing of Development Summary’ 
from the Flemington Local Area Plan.”     
  
The report by the Chief Executive which had been circulated was CONSIDERED. 
Following discussion, the report was AGREED. 
It was then proposed by Councillor T. O’Leary and seconded by Councillor A. 
Tormey: 
  
“This Council resolves to maintain the density that was envisaged for the Local 
Area Plan lands, as set out in the Draft Plan published in September 2024. “ 
  
The report by the Chief Executive which had been circulated was CONSIDERED. 
Following discussion, the report was AGREED. 
  
It was then proposed by Councillor T. O’Leary and seconded by Councillor A. 
Tormey: 
  

12 
 
“This Council resolves not to support the Chief Executive’s proposed Objective 5.14 
of the LAP, which states:  
“Proposals 
for residential development within each character area will be required to 
accord with the density ranges as set out for Key Town/ Large Town 
(Suburban/Urban Extension) within the Sustainable Residential Development and 
Compact Settlements – Guidelines for Planning Authorities (or any update 
thereof)”. “ 
  
The report by the Chief Executive which had been circulated was CONSIDERED. 
Following discussion, the report was AGREED. 
  
  
It was then proposed by Councillor B. McDonagh, seconded by Councillor M. 
McCamley and RESOLVED that- 
  
“That the Flemington Local Area Plan is hereby ADOPTED.”
