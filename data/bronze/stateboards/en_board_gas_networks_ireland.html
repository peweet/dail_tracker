Gas Networks Ireland

All Boards – Department of Housing, Local Government and Heritage – Gas Networks Ireland

Legal basis: Section 7A of the Gas Regulation Act 2013 (as amended)
Maximum Number of Positions: 9
Gender Balance Numbers: Female (5), Male (4)
Gender Balance Percentage: Female (56%), Male (44%)
Notes: Under the Act, the Board shall consist of at least 5 and not more than 9 directors (including its chairperson and the chief executive officer); the Chief Executive Officer is an ex officio Director. The Gas (Amendment) and Miscellaneous Provisions Act 2024 provided for the integration of Ervia into Gas Networks Ireland and it provided a primary legislative basis for appointments to the GNI Board.
