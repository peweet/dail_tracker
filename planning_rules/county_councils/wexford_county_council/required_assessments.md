# Required Assessments / Triggers — Wexford County Council

**Plan:** Wexford County Development Plan 2022-2028 — Volume 2: Development Management Manual
**Fetch date:** 2026-06-13 · Source: `raw/dm_standards.pdf`

The DM Manual enumerates the assessment triggers directly (§1.5):

- **Appropriate Assessment (AA)** — §1.5.1. Applications undergo **AA screening** to ensure no likely significant effects on the integrity (structure and function) of any **Natura 2000 site(s)**; full AA where effects cannot be excluded.
- **Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA / EIAR)** — §1.5.2. EIA carried out for development types/scales above the thresholds; **sub-threshold EIA** where warranted; an **EIAR** is required where EIA applies.
- **Traffic & Transport Assessment (TTA)** — Tables 6-1 (mandatory thresholds), 6-2 (advisory thresholds — national roads), 6-3 (sub-threshold criteria). TTA required above the tabulated thresholds.
- **Architectural Heritage / Protected Structures** — §7.2 (Protected Structures), §7.3 (Architectural Conservation Areas). Unauthorised works to / demolition of a protected structure are addressed; AHIA expectation for works affecting protected structures/curtilage.
- **Flood Risk** — flood-risk management applies per the 2009 Guidelines (Justification Test for development in flood zones).

> Wexford's DM Manual is one of the more explicit on triggers (numeric TTA threshold tables in Section 6). Recorded as stated; no inference applied.
