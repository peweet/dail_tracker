# Mayo County Council — Development Management Standards

**Authority:** Mayo County Council
**Plan:** Mayo County Development Plan 2022-2028 (came into effect 10 August 2022; interim plan following Ministerial Direction)
**Chapter/Volume:** Volume 2 — Development Management Standards
**Source URL:** https://consult.mayo.ie/en/consultation/notice-draft-ministerial-direction-matter-section-31-planning-development-act-2000-amended-mayo/chapter/volume-2-development-management-standards
**Direct PDF:** https://www.mayo.ie/getmedia/b8a9428e-ef09-4c5e-8780-e06eff7e6516/Vol-2-Development-Management-Standards-Mayo-CDP-22-28.pdf
**Fetch date:** 2026-06-13

> Only standards stated in the plan are recorded below. No inference applied.

## Residential Density (Table 2 — Maximum Residential Density Requirements)
- Town Centre / Adjacent: 35 dwelling units/hectare (14/acre)
- Inner Urban Suburbs: 20 dwelling units/hectare (8/acre)
- Urban Periphery / Rural Settlements: 5 dwelling units/hectare (2/acre)

## Rural One-Off Housing — Minimum Site Size (Section 2.3)
- Rural Countryside / Rural Transition Zones: 2000 m²
- Coastal / Lakeshore / Beaches: 3000 m², minimum 40 m width
- Replacement dwellings (exception): minimum 1500 m²

## Separation / Privacy Distances (Section 4.5)
- Opposing first-floor windows: minimum 22 metres
- Living-room windows: minimum 35 metres separation
- Side-elevation windows: staggered, with screen fence of at least 2 metres
- Side walls between dwellings: minimum 3 m
- Rear garden depth: minimum 11 m

## Private Open Space (Section 4.8)
- 1-2 bed houses: 55 m²
- 3-bed houses: 75 m²
- 4+ bed houses: 100 m²

## Public Open Space (Section 4.7)
- Greenfield sites: minimum 15% of total site area
- Brownfield / infill: minimum 10%

## Plot Ratio (Table 3 — Section 5.4)
- Commercial inside town centre: 1.5
- Commercial outside town centre: 1.0
- Industrial inside / outside: 0.5 / 1.0

## Site Coverage (Section 5.5)
- Shall not normally exceed 60%

## Access Visibility (Table 4 — Section 7.6) — X / Y / Z
- Urban 30 km/h: X = 2.4 m, Y = 23 m, Z = 23 m
- Regional 70 km/h: X = 3 m, Y = 90 m, Z = 120 m
- National 80 km/h: X = 3 m, Y = 100 m, Z = 160 m

## Building Setbacks from Roads (Section 7.11)
- National Roads: 40 m
- Regional Roads: 20 m
- Local Roads: 10 m

## Access Gradients (Table 6 — Section 7.9)
- Single houses dwelling area: +/- 2.5% over 10 m length
- Direct access: +/- 8% (absolute maximum +/- 10%)

## Car Parking (Table 7 — Section 7.12.1)
Residential:
- 2-bed: 1 space + 1 visitor per unit
- 3-bed: 2 spaces + 1 visitor per unit
- 1-bed apartment: 1 space + 1 visitor per unit
Commercial:
- Shopping centres: 1 space/25 m² (town), 1 space/20 m² (suburb)
- Retail warehousing: 1 space/30 m²
- Offices: 1 space/45 m²
- Restaurants: 1 space/10 m²

## Electric Vehicle Charging (Section 7.12.1)
- Minimum 10% of spaces with charging connections
- Ducting for 20% of car parking spaces

## Disabled Parking (Table 8 — Section 7.12.2)
- 5-25 spaces: 1 disabled space; 26-50: 3; 51-75: 4
- Disabled bay dimensions: 3 m wide × 4.75 m long

## Bicycle Parking (Table 9 — Section 7.12.3)
- Housing: 1 private secure bicycle space per bed space (minimum 2) plus 1 visitor space per two housing units
- Offices: 10% of employee numbers
- Shops: 1 stand per till/check-out
- Off-street car parks: 10% of total car spaces, minimum 50 spaces

## Bin Storage (Section 4.11)
- Within 50 metres of all residences; not immediately adjacent to front door or ground-floor windows

## Petrol Filling Station (Table 10 — Section 7.13), National/Regional Road
- Frontage width: 80 m; access width: 6 m; distance between access points: 30 m
- Front wall to pump island: 10 m; pump island width: 1.2 m; between pump islands: 6 m
