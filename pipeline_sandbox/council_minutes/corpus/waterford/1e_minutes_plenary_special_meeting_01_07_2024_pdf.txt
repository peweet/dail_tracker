1. To consider the recommendation from Public Appointments Service and the 
Corporate Policy Group in relation to the appointment of the Chief Executive of 
Waterford City & County Council, in accordance with the provisions of Local 
Government (Appointment of Chief Executive) Regulations 2014 
 
P. Johnston, A/DoS said the members of the Corporate Policy Group resolved to accept the 
recommendation from the Public Appointments Service of Mr. Sean McKeown for the position of 
Chief Executive and the CPG are recommending the approval of his appointment to the Council. The 
Council may decide to appoint, not to appoint or seek additional information from PAS. A decision 
not to appoint must be on grounds which are consistent with selection criteria and key requirements 
for the post.  
 
Cllr. Geoghegan proposed that the recommended candidate be appointed, this was seconded by Cllr. 
Kelly and agreed by all. 
 
Cllr. Kelly enquired as to the term of office.  
Cllr. Phelan asked the date on which the new CE would take up his appointment.  
Cllr. Roche asked if the Council could have sight of his presentation to PAS. 

 
2
Cllr. Conway enquired, given that Waterford has a Gaeltacht area, if Mr. McKeown had a working 
knowledge of the Irish Language.  
 
P. Johnston, A/DoS said the term of office is 7 years. 
H. Dunphy, MA said a planning day for the Members is scheduled for the end of July to which the 
new CE has been invited to attend. There is no start date as yet and she has no knowledge of his use 
of the Irish Language but this can be addressed at the planning day. 
 
The Mayor thanked the Members and said everybody is looking forward to working with Mr. 
McKeown. 
 
 
This concluded the business of the meeting.

Resolution of the Elected Members  
Waterford City and County Council, Plenary Council 
1st July 2024 
Ref: 2024/16 
 
 
As per Section 3 of the LOCAL GOVERNMENT (APPOINTMENT OF CHIEF EXECUTIVE) 
REGULATIONS 2014 and following the recommendation of the Corporate Policy Group the members of 
Waterford City & County Council decided to appoint the recommended person, Mr. Sean McKeown as Chief 
Executive for Waterford City and County Council.   
CERTIFIED RESOLUTION OF THE Plenary Council of Waterford City & County Council that Mr. Sean 
McKeown by appointed as Chief Executive of Waterford City & County Council.  Proposed Cllr. Damien 
Geoghegan, seconded by Cllr. Joe Kelly and agreed unanimously.
