# Development Management Standards — Offaly County Council

**Authority:** Offaly County Council
**Authority type:** County Council
**Plan:** Offaly County Development Plan 2021-2027 (adopted 10 September 2021; effective 22 October 2021)
**Chapter:** Chapter 13 — Development Management Standards
**Source URL:** https://www.offaly.ie/app/uploads/Council/Council_Services_A-Z/Planning_Building/Chapter-13-Development-Management-Standards.pdf
**Fetch date:** 2026-06-13

> All figures below are quoted verbatim from the source chapter. No inference.

## Residential Density
- The chapter (DMS-02 Density) does not tabulate dwellings-per-hectare ranges directly; it determines density "with reference to" the Sustainable Residential Development in Urban Areas Guidelines (2009), Sustainable Urban Housing: Design Standards for New Apartments (2020), Urban Development and Building Heights Guidelines (2018), and "the prevailing scale and pattern of development in the area." (No numeric uph table in chapter.)

## Separation / Privacy Distances
- **DMS-13 Separation Distances/Overlooking:** "A separation distance of a minimum of 22 metres between directly opposing rear first floor windows shall be observed" (may be relaxed in village/town centre with high quality design + alternative privacy provision).
- **DMS-14 Space around Buildings:** "A minimum distance of 2.5m for single storey, and 3m for two-storey buildings... between the side walls of adjacent dwellings or dwelling blocks with each building having a minimum of 1 metre to the boundary."
- **DMS-15 Side Windows:** Ground floor gable/side windows more than "6 metres from a properly screened common boundary may be permitted."

## Private Open Space — Houses (DMS-18)
| Unit type | Minimum |
|---|---|
| One bedroom | 48 m² |
| Two bedroom | 55 m² |
| Three bedroom | 60 m² |
| Four bedrooms or more | 75 m² |
(Note: source table layout pairs labels/values imperfectly; values are 48 / 55 / 60 / 75 m² ascending.)

## Private Open Space — Apartments (DMS-19, per 2020 Apartment Guidelines)
- Studio: 4 m²; One bed: 5 m²; Two bed (3 person): 6 m²; Two bed (4 person): 7 m²; Three bed: 9 m².

## Public Open Space (DMS-21)
- Greenfield sites: minimum **15% of total site area**.
- Brownfield / large infill sites: minimum **10% of total site area**.
- Provided within **150 metres walking distance** of every house.
- SuDS may form a maximum of **10%** of total open space provision.
- Narrow tracts **less than 10 metres in width** not counted as useable open space.
- Hierarchy of open space required for developments **greater than 20 houses**.

## Apartment Standards (DMS-08, 2020 Guidelines SPPRs)
- SPPR 3 minimum floor areas: Studio (1p) 37 m²; 1-bed (2p) 45 m²; 2-bed (4p) 73 m²; 3-bed (5p) 90 m².
- SPPR 5: Ground-level floor-to-ceiling height minimum **2.7m**.
- SPPR 6: Maximum **12 apartments per floor per stair/lift core**.
- SPPR 4 dual aspect: min 33% (central/accessible); generally min 50% (suburban/intermediate).

## Boundary Treatment (DMS-17)
- Front boundaries: capped walls/fences maximum **0.5 metres** high (unless open plan).
- Side boundaries of rear gardens: **1.8 metres to 2 metres** in height.

## Urban Infill / Setbacks (DMS-10)
- Street-edge setbacks should be minimised "for example 0-3m"; entrances "every 5-10 metres."
- Residential interface privacy setback "for example 1-3 metres."

## Refuse / Bin Storage (DMS-16)
- Communal bin storage located within **50 metres** of all residences; screened/ventilated; not adjacent to front door/ground-floor windows.

## Riparian Zones (DMS-25)
- Minimum **10 metre** riparian buffer strip either side of all watercourses (from top of bank), free of development.

## Rural One-Off House Standards (Open Countryside)
- **DMS-45 Site Size:** "A minimum site size of 0.2 hectares (0.5 acres) is required to accommodate a dwelling in the open countryside."
- **DMS-46 Road Frontage:** "minimum of 30 metres road frontage" (case-by-case relaxation).
- **DMS-54 Rural Infill:** site area minimum **0.202 hectares (0.5 acres)**.
- **DMS-57 Domestic Garages/Stores:** max floor area **100 m²**, max height **5 metres** in open countryside.

## Sightlines / Safe Sight Distances (DMS-97)
Sight distances from vehicular entrances:
- Local Tertiary Roads: **60 metres**
- Local Secondary Roads: **90 metres**
- Local Primary Roads: **120 metres**
- Regional Roads: **150 metres**
- National Roads: **230 metres**
- Measured "from a point 2.4 metres from the road edge at the proposed access to a point at the near edge of the approaching carriageway."

## Distance from Junctions (DMS-98) — new rural entrances
- Local Road junction: **50 metres**; Regional Road junction: **100 metres**; National Road junction: **150 metres**.

## Cycle Parking Standards (DMS-99) — selected
- Apartment: 1 space per bedroom (1 for studio) + 1 visitor space per 2 apartments.
- Offices/Professional: 10% of employee numbers or 1 per 10 car spaces (greater).
- Retail (Convenience & Comparison): 1 stand per 100 m² publicly accessible floor area.
- Schools (Towns): 20% of pupil registration or min 20; (Villages/Sráids/countryside) 10% or min 10.
- Business & Tech Park: 1 per 200 m²; Industry/Warehousing: 1 stand per 400 m².

## Cycle Parking Dimensions (DMS-100)
- Cycle stand **2.0 m x 1.0 m**, placed at least **1.0 m apart**.

## Car Parking Dimensions (DMS-101)
- Car parking bay: **5 m x 2.5 m**.
- Accessible/disability space: **5 m x 3.75 m**.
- Loading bay: **6 m x 3 m**.
- Circulation aisles: **6 m wide**.
- Cyclist headroom in multi-storey car parks: min **2.4m**.

## Car Parking Requirements (DMS-102) — maximum provision, selected
- House (outside town centre): 2 spaces per unit.
- House (town/village centre): 1 space per unit.
- Apartment: 1 space per unit + 1 visitor per 4 apartments.
- Housing for Older People: 1 space per unit.
- Hotel/Guesthouse: 1 per bedroom + 1 per 25 m² function space.
- Nursing Home/retirement: 1 per 4 beds.
- Convenience retail: 1 per 20 m² GFA; Comparison: 1 per 30 m².
- Retail Warehousing: 1 per 30 m² publicly accessible.
- Office/professional: 1 per 20 m²; Industry: 1 per 50 m²; Warehousing: 1 per 200 m².
- Hospital: 1.5 per bed; Crèche: 1 per 4 children + 1 per employee; Schools: 2 per classroom.
- Minimum **5% of car parking spaces shall be accessible spaces**.

## Electric Vehicle Charging (DMS-104)
- Minimum **10%** of required car parking spaces to be provided with electrical connection points for functional EV charging; remaining spaces fitted with ducting for future fit-out.

## Plot Ratio / Site Coverage / Building Heights
- Not tabulated numerically in this chapter; building heights governed by DMS-09 (refers to Urban Development and Building Heights Guidelines 2018, SPPRs 1-4) and Chapter 7 opportunity sites (Tullamore).

## Other Quantitative Items
- Pre-planning consultation mandatory: residential >10 housing units or non-residential >1,000 m² gross floor space (also DMS / s.247).
- Design Statement (DMS-07): required for residential developments in excess of 10 units (urban).
- Access Statement (DMS-06): housing developments of 5 houses or more.
- Green Infrastructure Masterplan (DMS-22): 20 houses or more.
- Phasing programme (DMS-28): 50 dwellings or more.
- Childcare (DMS-32): one facility (≈20 places) per 75 houses.
- Home-based economic activity (DMS-63): change of use not to exceed 25% of dwelling floor area.
- Hot food takeaways (DMS-64): not permitted within 200 metres radius of educational establishments/parks.
- Service station shop net floor space (DMS-61): not to exceed 100 m².
- Retail warehousing ancillary products (DMS-59): not exceed 20% of total retail floorspace.
- Sráid (village street) residential (DMS-35): site size of 0.5 acres; max 4 houses; tree replacement ratio 2:1.
- Business/Tech Park unit building line (DMS-73): generally not less than 15 metres from road + 5 metre planted strip.
- Green Roof (DMS-72): minimum 60% of roof area for roofs greater than 300 m².
