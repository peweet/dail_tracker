## Appropriate Assessment / Habitats Directive (Natura 2000 / SAC / SPA)
- Habitats Directive Assessment (HDA / Appropriate Assessment) required under Habitats Directive 92/43/EEC. Plans/projects with potential to impact Natura 2000 sites must be screened for AA; a full AA carried out unless screening establishes no significant effect. Prepared per Article 6(3)/6(4) and "Appropriate Assessment of Plans and Projects in Ireland — Guidance for Planning Authorities" (DEHLG, 2009).

## Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA / EIAR)
- EIAR required for development of a class in Schedule 5 of the Planning and Development Regulations 2001 (as amended) exceeding the limit/quantity/threshold set for that class.
- Sub-threshold EIA: required where the planning authority considers the proposal likely to have significant effects on the environment (regard to Schedule 7 and DHPLG 2018 EIA Guidelines).

## Flood Risk Assessment / Justification Test
- Compliance with "The Planning System and Flood Risk Assessment Guidelines" (DEHLG & OPW, 2009) required.
- Development on previously developed lands within **Flood Zones A or B** subject to site-specific flood risk assessment with structural/non-structural measures (floor levels, internal layout, flood-resistant/resilient construction, emergency response, access/egress).

## Architectural Heritage / Protected Structures / ACA
- Particular consideration for buildings on the Record of Protected Structures (RPS), within Architectural Conservation Areas (ACAs), on the NIAH, and within Primary Retail Areas (PRAs) — applies to shopfronts, security shutters, satellite dishes, murals/advertising. (Built heritage policy in Volume 1 Chapter 13.) Architectural Heritage Protection Guidelines (DAHG, 2011) applied.

## Traffic & Transport Assessment (TTIA)
- TTIA required (per TII Traffic and Transport Assessment Guidelines 2014) for:
  - Residential development over **200 dwellings**
  - Retail/Leisure over **1,000 sqm**
  - Industrial over **5,000 sqm**
  - Office/Hospital/Educational over **2,500 sqm**
  - Distribution/Warehousing over **10,000 sqm**
  - Where there is a **10%** traffic increase (**5%** in congested areas).
- Road Safety Audit: required where significant impact on road safety; for new residential development of **20 units or more** (carried out by TII-approved assessors).
- Mobility Management Plan: workplaces over **100 employees**; all new schools or existing schools with **20%+** classroom expansion (thresholds in Table 6.3).

## Site suitability / wastewater / percolation
- New rural houses: on-site wastewater per EPA Code of Practice for Domestic Wastewater Treatment Systems (EPA, 2021); site assessor report submitted with application.
- Commercial onsite wastewater with Population Equivalent > **10**: EPA Wastewater Treatment Manual for Small Communities (EPA, 1999).

## Other statements / assessments
- Sustainability Statement: residential **10+** units; non-residential > **1,000 m²** GFA or **20+** employees.
- Construction Environmental Management Plan (CEMP): industrial/office/retail/warehouse/business park on sites > **0.5 ha**; or **5+** residential units.
- SUDS assessment with all new development applications.
- Noise Impact Assessment: for noise-generating commercial/industrial development, within the Noise Action Plan Action Planning Area, or near major roads (> 3 million vehicles/annum).
- Development Impact Assessment: for tourism-related developments (where indicated).
- Landscape and Visual Impact Assessment + restoration bond: for quarries/extractive development.
