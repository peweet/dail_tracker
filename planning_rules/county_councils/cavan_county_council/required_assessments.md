# Required Assessments — Cavan County Council

**Authority:** Cavan County Council
**Plan:** Cavan County Development Plan 2022-2028
**Chapter:** Chapter 13 — Development Management (with flood content in Climate/Water chapters)
**Source:** Written Statement PDF — https://www.cavancoco.ie/file-library/planning/development-plans/development-plan-2022-2028/written-statement/cdp-written-statement.pdf
**Fetch date:** 2026-06-13

## Appropriate Assessment (AA)
Screening for Appropriate Assessment of implications on Natura 2000 sites (SACs/SPAs) is required for all relevant proposals. If screening determines AA is required, the applicant must prepare a Natura Impact Statement (Stage 2 AA). Required under EU Habitats Directive (92/43/EEC) and Birds Directive (79/409/EEC). Note: certain peat/land works exceeding 0.1 hectares require planning permission and AA where necessary.

## Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA / EIAR)
Planning and Development Regulations 2001 (as amended) specify mandatory thresholds above which an EIAR is required, by type and scale of development. Where the Planning Authority considers a sub-threshold proposal likely to have significant environmental effects, a sub-threshold/discretionary EIAR can be requested by notice in writing.

## Architectural Heritage Impact Assessment
Not enumerated as a specific quantitative trigger in Chapter 13. Protected structures / ACAs are addressed through the Heritage chapter and the Development Management chapter; assessment of impact on protected structures/ACAs is a design consideration but no fixed numeric trigger is stated.

## Flood Risk Assessment (FRA) / Justification Test
- Site-specific FRA required for ALL planning applications in areas at risk of flooding (fluvial, coastal, pluvial or groundwater), even for development appropriate to the flood zone.
- Development inappropriate to a Flood Zone requires a Justification Test AND a detailed FRA.
- Sequential approach applied where only part of a site is at risk.
- FRA must address climate-change implications; carried out per The Planning System and Flood Risk Management Guidelines (2009) and Circular PL2/2014 (as updated).
- Flood Zone C (probability <0.1%): site-specific FRA may be required.
- Flood mitigation likely to affect European sites downstream undergoes environmental assessment and AA.

## Traffic & Transport Assessment
Referenced under transport objectives; required where developments generate significant traffic. (Chapter 7 transport — no fixed numeric trip threshold quoted in DM chapter.)

## Site Suitability / Percolation / Wastewater (one-off rural houses)
Rural dwellings require acceptable wastewater disposal arrangements and safe access. Private wastewater treatment plants must be operated in compliance with EPA standards. (Site suitability/percolation tests per EPA Code of Practice apply; detailed test specifics are in rural housing assessment criteria, Chapter 12.) Minimum 50 m buffer from smaller wastewater treatment plants (greater for WWTPs >1500 PE).
