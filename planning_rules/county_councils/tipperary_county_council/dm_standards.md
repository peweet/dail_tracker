# Development Management Standards

**Authority:** Tipperary County Council
**Authority type:** County Council
**Plan:** Tipperary County Development Plan 2022-2028 (made 11 July 2022; effective 22 August 2022)
**Chapter/Source:** Volume 3, Appendix 6 — Development Management Standards
**Source URL:** https://www.tipperarycoco.ie/sites/default/files/2022-09/Volume%203%20Development%20Management%20Standards.pdf
**Fetch date:** 2026-06-13

> All figures below are quoted directly from the source document. No inference.

## Residential density
- Density: "Please refer to the relevant settlement plan for guidance" (Table 4.1). Numeric densities are set per-settlement in Volume 2, not in this DM Standards volume.

## Public open space
- At least **15%** of total site area shall be allocated for public open space (Table 4.1).

## Private open space (rear garden, behind front building line)
- **48 sqm** for 1-2 bedroom houses
- **60 sqm** for 3-5 bedroom houses
- **75 sqm** for 5+ bedroom houses

## Separation / privacy distances
- Minimum **22 m** separation between directly opposing windows at first floor level (may be reduced for high-quality/innovative design at Council's discretion).

## Rural one-off house — minimum site area
- Not specified as a numeric minimum site area in this volume. Rural one-off house design is governed by the "Rural Housing Design Guideline" (Volume 3, separate appendix) and on-site wastewater per EPA Code of Practice for Domestic Wastewater Treatment Systems (EPA, 2021); a site assessor report is required.

## Building heights
- Governed by "Urban Development and Building Heights, Guidelines for Planning Authorities (DHPLG, 2018)" (incl. SPPRs 1-4) and Chapter 5; no fixed numeric height table in this volume.

## Domestic / ancillary standards
- Detached garages: should be **less than 70 sqm**.
- Detached home-office / habitable room (working from home): floor area shall **not exceed 20 sqm**.
- Ancillary family accommodation extension: shall not exceed gross floor area of **50 sqm**; max 1 bedroom (2 in exceptional cases).

## Car parking standards (Table 6.4 — Minimum Car Parking Standards)
- Each car space: **4.8 m x 2.5 m**, with **6.1 m** wide circulation aisles.
- Disabled spaces: **3 m** wide; provision **1 per 20** spaces.
- Motorcycle parking: **10%** of total car parking spaces for all new development.
- Dwelling (up to 2 bedrooms): **1 space** per dwelling unit.
- Dwelling (3+ bedrooms): **2 spaces** per dwelling unit.
- Multi-residential: 1-2 bed = 1 space + 2 visitor spaces per 5 units; 3+ bed = 2 spaces/unit + 2 visitor spaces per 5 units.
- Churches/Theatres/Cinema/Auditoriums: 1 space per 5 seats.
- Bars/Restaurant: 0.5 per staff + 1 per 15 sq.m public area.
- Clinics/Surgeries/Small medical: 1 per staff + 2 per consulting room.
- Caravan Park (mobile homes): 1 per staff + 2 per unit of accommodation.
- Campsite: 1 per pitch.
- Crèches: 1 per staff + 1 per 5 children.
- Dance Halls/Discos: 0.5 per staff + 1 per 10 sq.m.
- Golf Courses: 0.5 per staff + 4 per hole.
- Hotel/Motel/Guest House: 0.5 per staff + 1 per guestroom (public areas per relevant table standard).
- Leisure Centre: 0.5 per staff + 1 per 50 sq.m.
- Light Industry: 1 car space + 1 HGV space per 35 sqm.
- Manufacturing: 0.5 per staff + 1 per 35 sqm + 1 HGV space.
- Nursing Homes: 1 per staff + 1.5 per patient bed.
- Hospital (general): 1.5 per patient bed; outpatient 1 per staff + 2 per consulting room.
- Offices (banks etc): 0.5 per staff + 1 per 35 sq.m.
- Retail Shops/Supermarkets: 0.5 per staff + 1 per 20 sqm + 1 HGV space per 750 sqm GFA.
- Schools: 1 per teaching staff + 1 per classroom (plus set-down + bus parking).
- Colleges of further education: 1 per classroom + 1 per 5 students.
- Retail Warehousing: 1 per staff + 1 per 50 sq.m.
- Science & Technology / Business Park: 1 per staff + 1 per 25 sqm.
- Warehousing (non-retail): 1 per staff + 1 per 100 sqm.
- Playgrounds: 10 spaces per facility.
- Playing Fields: minimum 25 car spaces + 2 bus parking spaces.

## Bicycle parking (Table 6.5 — Minimum Bicycle Parking Standards)
- Residential dwelling/apartment: long-term 1 space per unit; visitor/short-stay 1 space per 5 units.
- Student accommodation: long-term 1 space per 2 bedrooms.
- Offices: long-term 1 space per 200 m² GFA.
- Industry/Warehousing, shops, hotels, etc.: 1 space per 5 staff (long-term) with various visitor ratios.
- Visitor cycle stands: frame-supporting stands set **1.2 m to 1.5 m** apart.
- Multi-storey car parks: 1 cycle space per 20 parking spaces.

## EV charging (Table 6.6 — applies S.I. 393/2021)
- Residential multi-unit (new + major renovation): minimum **1 EV charge point per 5 car parking spaces** (ducting for every space).
- New dwellings with in-curtilage parking: infrastructure/ducting to enable a recharging point.
- Non-residential (>10 spaces): at least 1 recharging point + minimum 1 per 5 spaces equipped.
- Publicly accessible (supermarket/cinema): at least 1 recharging point + minimum 1 per 5 spaces equipped.

## Loading
- Loading bays: generally **9 m x 5 m** (at least **6 m x 3 m**).

## Sightlines / visibility (Section 6.1)
- New direct accesses not permitted within **90 m** of a roundabout exit on a national road, or within **50 m** on a non-national road.
- Y-Distance measured to object height **1.05 m** above road surface.
- **Table 6.1 X-Distance:** National roads (simple junction, stop control) **2.4 m**; Regional & Local roads (stop control) **3.0 m**; Regional & Local (yield control) **4.5 m**; lightly trafficked single residence access **2.0 m**.
- **Table 6.2 Y-Distance (rural non-national road):** 50 km/h limit → **70 m**; 60 → **90 m**; 80 → **120 m**; 100 (limit) → **215 m** (urban Y-distances 33/45/59/72 m for 30-60 km/h). Minimum design speed: rural non-national **50 kph**, urban non-national **40 kph**.

## Motor fuel stations (Section 5.8)
- Net retail floor space shall not exceed **100 sqm**.
- Minimum frontage: **30 m** within 50/60 kph area; **45 m** in other speed-limit areas.
- Minimum **7 m** from pump island to road boundary.
- Access points **7-9 m** wide, minimum junction radius **10.7 m**.
- Minimum **50 m** from entrance to nearest major junction; **25 m** to nearest minor junction.
- Footpath **2 m** wide with **0.5 m** high wall along front boundary.

## Industrial development (Table 5.1)
- Access roads: minimum carriageway width **7.5 m** with **1.3 m** grass strip and **2 m** footpath(s).
- Turning space for **15 m** articulated and **9 m** fixed-axle vehicles.
- Building line set-back of at least **12 m** from estate road-side boundaries.

## Shopfronts / signage (Section 5.2)
- Hanging sign bracket: not more than **80 cm** from wall face; lowest part minimum **2.5 m** above pavement.
- Max **1** hanging sign per property frontage.

## Other / environmental
- Riparian buffer strip: minimum **10 m** either side of all watercourses (from top of bank), free of development.
- CEMP required: industrial/office/retail/warehouse/business park on sites > **0.5 ha**; or developments of **5+** residential units.
- Sustainability Statement required: residential **10+** units; non-residential > **1,000 m²** GFA or **20+** employees.
- Public art: encouraged for residential developments over **100 units**.
- Noise Impact Assessment for development near major roads (traffic > **3 million vehicles per annum**).
