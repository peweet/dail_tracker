NOTICE SUBMITTED UNDER SECTION 44 (6) OF THE LOCAL GOVERNMENT 
ACT (2001) 
 
An Chomhairle considered the following as submitted under Section 44 (6) of the Local 
Government Act (2001):- 
 
1) The Capital Acquisitions Programme 2024 
2) Tenant in Situ Programme 2025 
3) The 33 households that had commenced the Tenant in Situ process 
4) Other schemes, including Rightsizing, that fall under the Capital Acquisitions 
Programme 
5) Homeless Prevention measures and exits from homelessness  
 
 
 
 

An Chomhairle considered and approved the following motion:  
 
‘That Cork City Council will write to the Minister for Housing and the Taoiseach requesting 
urgent increased funding for the Capital Acquisitions Scheme 2025 to prevent families and 
individuals from being homeless and to allow the scheme to function as previously, given its 
success.’ 
 
 
 
This concluded the business of the meeting
