F/482/25 
   (Agenda Item No 18) 
Chief Executive's Report 
 
The report by the Chief Executive, which had been circulated, was CONSIDERED. 
  
The Mayor advised the Members that a motion has been received. 
  
The following motion was proposed by Councillor J. Burtchaell and seconded by 
Councillor H. Redwood: 
  
“That the Chief Executive and Housing & Community Development Dept note the 
increasing levels of private tenancy Notice of Terminations (NoT's) being issued 
in the county with the Council informed of 105 NoT's for the Dublin 15 area 
alone year to date, recognising that this will inevitably result in increasing 
numbers of homeless families given the dire shortage of alternative private 
rented accommodation or even emergency accommodation in some cases and 
work to develop a response to address this intensifying crisis.” 
  
Following discussion the motion was AGREED. 
  
The Mayor advised the Members that an additional motion had been received 
and was ruled out of order. 
  
  
The Chief Executive’s report was NOTED.
