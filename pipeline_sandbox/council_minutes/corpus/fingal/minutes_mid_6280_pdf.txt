   (Agenda Item No 31) 
Councillor M. Quinn - The Pausing of Council Rent Increases 
 

13 
 
The motion, in the name of Councillor M. Quinn was proposed by Councillor M. 
Quinn and seconded by Councillor B. McDonagh. 
  
Following discussions, it was AGREED to extend the meeting for 15 minutes. 
  
During the course of the discussion, the Mayor informed the members an 
amended motion was received. 
  
The Mayor then instructed that a roll call vote be taken to accept the amended 
motion and on a division the voting resulted as follows: 
  
FOR:               20       TWENTY 
AGAINST:     5          FIVE 
ABSTAIN:      9          NINE 
  
For: Councillors Cathal Boland, Darragh Butler, Eimear Carbone-Mangan, Luke 
Corkery, Kieran Dennison, Eoghan Dockrell, Jimmy Guerin, Cathal Haughey, Joan 
Hopkins, Darren Jack Kelly, Tom Kitt, Ted Leddy, Grainne Maguire, Paul Mulville, 
Tony Murphy, Joe Newman, Eoghan O'Brien, Tom O'Leary, JK Onwumereh, 
Aoibhinn Tormey. 
  
AGAINST: Councillors John Burtchaell, Angela Donnelly, Breda Hanaphy, 
Malachy Quinn, John Smyth. 
  
ABSTAIN: Councillors Mark Boland, James Humphreys, Corina Johnston, Mary 
McCamley, Brian McDonagh, Patrick Quinlan, Gerard Sheehan, Ellen Troy, John 
Walsh. 
  
The Mayor declared the vote PASSED.  
  
The amended motion was proposed by Councillor T. Murphy and second by 
Councillor D.J. Kelly: 
  
Insert words after increases “To support OAP’s and People on disability 
payments that are in the lowest income stream” 
  
To read: 
  
“In light of the recent acceleration in the cost-of-living crisis, I am calling on this 
Council to support the pausing of Council rent increases, to support OAP’s and 
people on disability payments that are in the lowest income stream that will take 
effect this month, in conjunction with measures announced by the government 
in relation to fuel costs, transport cost and other associated costs.”

item.

On recommencement of the meeting, Councillor T. Murphy withdrew his motion.

submitted by Councillor M. Quinn and on a division the voting resulted as 
follows:

ABSTAIN:      0          ZERO
