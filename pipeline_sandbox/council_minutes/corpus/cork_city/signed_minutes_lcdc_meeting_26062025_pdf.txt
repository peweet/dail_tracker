5. Cork City LCDC Change of Membership 
Majo Rivas played a huge part in supporting the LCDC as Vice-Chair, SICAP Sub-Group Member 
and representing the Social Inclusion Pillar of the PPN on the LCDC. Majo has achieved a career 
move and in this context, she has resigned from the LCDC.    
The Cork City PPN being aware of this have nominated their replacement representative, 
Konrad Im, to join the Cork City LCDC in July 2025. Konrad’s nomination was put to the LCDC 
membership as below. 
 
Proposed by: 
Denis J. Fitzpatrick 
Seconded by: Michael Delargey 
 
Result: Membership of Konrad Im to Cork City LCDC was ratified by Cork City LCDC. 
 
Denis Leamy, Chairperson expressed his thanks and appreciation to Majo for her time and 
dedication to Cork City’s LCDC. He wished her well in her new role.
