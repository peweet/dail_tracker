# Leitrim County Council — Development Management Standards

**Authority:** Leitrim County Council
**Plan:** Leitrim County Development Plan 2023-2029

## Building Heights (13.9.1)
- Respect local context/streetscape; greater-than-prevailing heights need urban design rationale.
- Outside Carrick-on-Shannon: increased building height limited to 3-4 storeys.

## Site Coverage (13.9.2)
- Residential: max 50%; Industrial: 75%; Other employment: 60%; Retail & commercial: 66%; Town centre locations: generally up to 80%.

## Plot Ratio (Table 13.1)
- Town Centre / Brownfield: 1.0-2.0
- Suburban / Edge of Town Centre: 0.5-1.0
- Outer Edge of Town: 0.35-0.5

## Overlooking / Separation (13.9.4 / 13.10.3)
- Minimum 22 metres between opposing above-ground-floor windows for habitable rooms.
- Minimum 22 m separation between directly opposing first-floor habitable rooms (residential amenity).
- Minimum 2.0-2.5 m distance between detached and semi-detached dwellings.

## Residential Density (13.10.1)
- Indicative density in Table 2.6 (Chapter 2). Examples in plan: infill ~15 units/ha, edge of centre ~30 units/ha, outer suburban ~25 units/ha; Tier 2A Growth Towns 20 units/ha; Key Villages 15 units/ha; lower-density settlements 5-10 units/ha.

## Boundary Treatments (13.10.4)
- Private open space enclosure: 1.8 m - 2 m high solid block wall, capped and plastered both sides.
- Side boundaries between rear gardens: concrete post/base with timber panel, with 2 m length of 1.8-2 m block wall for first 2 m from rear building line.
- Side walls adjoining public footpath: max 1.2 m high to front building line (then 2 m).

## Private Open Space — Dwelling Houses (Table 13.3)
- One/Two Bedroom: 55 m²
- Three Bedroom: 60 m²
- Four Bedroom or more: 75 m²

## Apartment Minimum Area Standards (Table 13.4)
- Minimum floor areas, private open space and storage set out in Table 13.4 (per Apartment Guidelines; full figures in source).

## Public Open Space (13.10.7)
- Greenfield: minimum 15% of total site area
- Large infill / brownfield: minimum 10%
- Institutional lands: 20%
- Narrow tracts (<10 m width) not acceptable; SuDS max 10% of open-space provision.

## Residential Amenity — Bins (13.10.3)
- Storage for 3 wheelie bins; terraced/townhouses provide screened bin storage in planning application.

## Rural Housing (13.11)
- Design per "Design Guide for the Siting and Design of Single Houses within the Leitrim Countryside".
- Larger houses (e.g. in excess of 200 m²) should sub-divide mass into smaller elements.
- Application sites should ideally be flanked by minimum of two established natural boundaries.
- Hedgerow removal to achieve sightlines: minimum amount; new native hedgerow planted inside line of visibility.
- On-site wastewater per EPA Code of Practice for Domestic Waste Water Treatment Systems (p.e. <10) (2021).
- Note: Chapter 13 does not set a single numeric minimum site area for one-off houses; rural housing eligibility criteria are in Chapter 3, with site-specific design/percolation tests governing area.

## Distance of Entrances from Road Junctions (13.16.5)
- National Primary Roads: 150 m; National Secondary Roads: 100 m; Regional Roads: 100 m; Local Primary Roads: 40 m
- (No National Secondary Road currently in Co. Leitrim; objective to reclassify R280.)

## Minimum Sightline Requirements (Table 13.5) — by road status and speed (100/80/60 km/h)
- National Primary Roads & R280: 215 m / 215 m / 80 m
- Regionally & Locally Important Roads: 215 m / 160 m / 80 m
- Other Regional Roads: N/A / 160 m / 80 m
- County Roads: N/A / 120 m* / 70 m  (*90 m may be acceptable where operational speed ≤60 km/h)
- Measured at 1.05 m above ground from a point 3.0 m back from the edge of the carriageway to a point 260 mm over the road surface.

## Car (max) and Cycle (min) Parking Standards (Table 13.6)
- Dwelling House: 2 per unit (car) / 1 per unit (cycle)
- Apartment/Flat: 1.5 per unit / 1 per bedroom (studio)
- Hotels/B&B/Guesthouse/Hostel: 1 per bedroom / 1 per 20 beds
- Hostel: 1 per bedroom or 1 per 10-bed dormitories / 1 per 10 beds
- Food Retail: 1 per 20 m² GFA / 1 per 75 m²
- Non-Food Retail: 6 per 100 m² GFA / 1 per 100 m²
(Standards are maximum for car, minimum for cycle; full table in source.)

## Cycle Parking — Showers (13.16.7)
- Office: 250-500 m² = 1 shower; 500-1,000 m² = 2 showers; +1 shower per additional 1,000 m².
- Non-office: 1 shower for first 5 cycle spaces, +1 shower per 10 spaces thereafter.

## EV Charging
- Service stations / forecourt developments: EV charging point(s) to be provided and clearly demarcated (13.x petrol-station standards).
