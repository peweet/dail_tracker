ANNOUNCEMENT
At the start of the meeting, the Mayor, Councillor. A. Henchy reminded Members 
that Standing Orders would apply to all aspects of the meeting.
 
The Mayor, Councillor. A. Henchy observed the conclusion of the inquest into the 
Stardust tragedy and paid tribute to Mr John Colgan a Fingal resident who lived 
in Pinegrove in Swords, who lost his life in the tragedy.  The Mayor, Councillor. A. 
Henchy read into the record the testimony from the Stardust inquest.
 
The Mayor, Councillor. A. Henchy also paid tribute to Councillor. T. Doyle and her 
family members, who were physically and verbally assaulted and condemned 
the actions of the perpetrators.  The Mayor, Councillor. A. Henchy and all the 
Members wished Councillor. T. Doyle and her family a speedy recovery from 
their injuries.
