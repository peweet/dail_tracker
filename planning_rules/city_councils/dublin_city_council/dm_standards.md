> Note on scope: Dublin's Chapter 15 (Written Statement) states the residential/qualitative standards inline but **defers several quantitative standards to Appendices**: density, building height, plot ratio and site coverage to **Appendix 3**; car/cycle parking, road widths, traffic assessment thresholds to **Appendix 5**. The numbers below are those stated directly in Chapter 15 sub-sections. Items deferred to appendices are flagged.

## Residential — open space

| Standard | Value (minimum) |
|---|---|
| Public open space — residential (zones Z1, Z2, Z3, Z4, Z5, Z6, Z8, Z10, Z14) | 10% of overall site area (Table 15-4) |
| Public open space — residential (zones Z12, Z15) | 25% of site area |
| Private open space (houses) — general | 10 sq.m per bedspace (single bedroom = 1 bedspace, double = 2) |
| Private open space (houses) — inner city | 5–8 sq.m per bedspace |
| Rear garden (houses, general) | up to 60–70 sq.m considered sufficient |
| Financial contribution in lieu of open space | applies only to schemes of 9 or more units |

## Residential — separation / privacy

- Rear first-floor windows of opposing 2-storey dwellings: traditionally **~22 m** back-to-back (may be relaxed with design justification).

## Apartment standards (Section 15.9; per Sustainable Urban Housing: Design Standards for New Apartments, Dec 2020)

Minimum floor areas (Table 15-5):

| Unit type | Bedspaces | Minimum floor area |
|---|---|---|
| Studio | 1 | 37 sq.m |
| 1 bed | 2 | 45 sq.m |
| 2 bed | 4 | 73 sq.m |
| 3 bed | 5 | 90 sq.m |

Unit mix:
- SPPR1: up to 50% one-bedroom or studio units; of which no more than **20–25%** studios; no minimum requirement for 3+ bed units unless set by HNDA.
- North Inner City & Liberties Sub-City Areas (schemes of 15+ units): minimum **15%** three-or-more-bedroom units; maximum **25–30%** one-bed/studio units.

## Childcare

- One childcare facility (minimum **20 child spaces**) for every **75 dwelling units** in new mixed-use/residential schemes.
- Childcare needs audit assessed within a **1 km** radius.

## Community / social audit and schools

- Community & social audit required for residential schemes of **50 or more units**; assesses provision within a **750 m** radius.
- School-demand report required for applications over **50 dwellings**; very large schemes (**800+ units**) must phase dwelling completion with school provision.

## Play infrastructure

- Schemes of **25 or more units**: small play spaces of **85–100 sq.m** (toddlers / under-6).
- Schemes of **100 or more apartments**: additional play areas of **200–400 sq.m** for older children/teenagers.

## Design statements

- Architectural Design Statement required for **50+ residential units** (Table 15-2 sets information requirements); may also be required for large-scale commercial development.

## Density, height, plot ratio, site coverage

- **Deferred to Appendix 3.** Chapter 15 (15.5.4–15.5.6) states the policy approach (higher density at high-quality public-transport locations; brownfield/infill greater densities) but gives no in-chapter numeric figures.

## Car parking / cycle parking / road widths

- **Deferred to Appendix 5** (Access and Design Standards; Cycle and Car Parking Standards and Management; road/footpath design). No numeric ratios stated within Chapter 15 itself.

## EV charging / bin storage

- Not enumerated numerically in Chapter 15; addressed via national apartment standards and Appendix 5. No in-chapter figures captured.
