F/280/25 
   (Agenda Item No 26) 
Planning & Development Act 2000 Part XI (As Amended), Planning & 
Development Regulations 2001 Part 8 (As Amended), Public Realm 
Improvements and Active Travel measures for Skerries Harbour Road and 
Red Island in Skerries 
 
The report by the Chief Executive which had been circulated was CONSIDERED.  
A presentation was given to the Members by Ms. Sinead Murphy, Senior 
Engineer and Mr. Breen Doris, Senior Executive Engineer, Environment, Climate 
Action, Active Travel and Sport. 
  
Following discussions, the Mayor informed the members a motion was received. 
  
The motion in the name of Councillor B. Ryan was proposed by Councillor B. 
Ryan and seconded by Councillor M. Boland.  
  
“That this proposed Part 8 development does not proceed because of the 
adverse impacts it will have on businesses, residents, water-based clubs and 
organisations, less able consumers of hospitality, emergency services, and the 
negative environmental impact of the removal of precious green space to be 
replaced by parking spaces and a turning space for heavy goods vehicles, and 
that less impactful changes be considered as part of the overall active travel plan 
for Skerries which is currently underway.” 

8 
 
Following further discussion, the Mayor instructed that a roll call vote be taken 
and on a division the voting resulted as follows: 
  
FOR:             2        TWO 
AGAINST:     35      THIRTY FIVE 
ABSTAIN:      0        ZERO 
  
FOR:               Councillors Patrick Quinlan, Brendan Ryan.      
  
AGAINST:      Councillors Mark Boland, John Burtchaell, Darragh Butler, Eimear
