# Required Assessments / Triggers — Donegal County Council

**Plan:** County Donegal Development Plan 2024-2030 (DRAFT)
**Fetch date:** 2026-06-13 · Source: `raw/draft-county-donegal-development-plan-2024-2030.pdf`

- **Appropriate Assessment (AA)** — the Habitats Directive requires assessment of plans/projects likely to affect protected habitats/species (Natura 2000); a **Natura Impact Statement** may be required (e.g. for roads). Plan-level AA accompanies the draft.
- **Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)** — the EU EIA Directive requires assessment of projects above thresholds; the Council will **carry out EIA as necessary**; an Environmental Impact Statement (EIS)/EIAR may be required.
- **Flood Risk Assessment** — the Guidelines require **Strategic Flood Risk Assessments (SFRA)**; development must comply with the plan's Flood Risk Objectives and Policies (Justification Test in flood zones).
- **Architectural Heritage** — architectural heritage treated as a non-renewable resource requiring careful management; protection of protected structures (AHIA expectation for affecting works).
- **Single-house effluent (rural)** — catchment-specific controls (RH-P-10) on single-house treatment-system effluent, with environmental considerations.

> Triggers sit mainly in the environment/heritage and rural-housing chapters; recorded as referenced in the draft, without inference on thresholds. Re-verify on adoption.
