C/438/24 
ADJOURNMENT OF THE MEETING  
Cllr McBrearty expressed concern that he had not been contacted in 
relation to the pre-budget meetings. He said that he should have been 
afforded the courtesy of taking part in the discussion process given the 
mandate he had been given to represent the electorate of the 
Lifford/Stranorlar Municipal District.  
 
On the proposal of Cllr C Brogan, seconded by Cllr McDermott, it was 
resolved to adjourn the meeting to 3.30p.m. to facilitate further discussion 
on the 2025 budgetary provisions.    
 
C/439/24 
RESUMPTION OF THE MEETING  
A further adjournment was proposed by Cllr McGowan, seconded by Cllr 
McGarvey to adjourn the meeting until 4.30p.m.  
 
C/440/24 
CONSIDERTION OF 2025 BUDGET  
The meeting recommenced at 4.42pm with Cllr MacGiolla Easbuig 
contending that he also had not been invited to take part in any of the pre-
budget negotiations. He said that there had been no issue, a number of 
months ago regarding the approval of the LPT which impacted many

families and ordinary working people, but that when business or enterprise 
was affected, there was a reluctance to approve any increases. 
 
On the proposal of Cllr Blaney, seconded by Cllr McGowan it was 
resolved to adjourn the meeting until 3pm on Thursday 12th December, 
2024 to facilitate further discussions on the 2025 budgetary provisions.
