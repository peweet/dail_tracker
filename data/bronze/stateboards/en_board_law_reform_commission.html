All Boards – Office of the Attorney General – Law Reform Commission

Legal basis: Law Reform Commission Act 1975, s. 3
Maximum Number of Positions: 5
Gender Balance Numbers: Female (2), Male (3)
Gender Balance Percentage: Female (40%), Male (60%)
Notes: The Act requires that Commissioners are persons appearing to the Government to be suitably qualified by the holding of judicial office, by experience as a barrister or solicitor or as a teacher of law or by reason of such other special experience, qualification or training as, in the opinion of the Government, is appropriate having regard to the functions of the Commission.
