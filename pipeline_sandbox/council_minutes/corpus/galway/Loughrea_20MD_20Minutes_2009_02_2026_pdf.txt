3.NOTICES OF MOTIONS RECEIVED
LMD2470
Cllr. Geraldine Donohue:
To ask Galway County Council to clean, repair and maintain the footpath put in place by
Galway County Council on the R380 (N66) from Gort town to Fahys Cross.
Reply:
This matter is regularly brought to our attention but, as you are aware, this office has
limited access to funding to address footpath repairs outside of certain specific funding
streams.The limited discretionary funding that is available to us is generally targeted on
a prioritised basis i.e. appropriateness of the location - is it within a reduced speed limit
zone, is public lighting available, number of potential users, number of benefitting
individuals, age profile of the potential users. Existing footpaths outside of the reduced
speed limits are legacy works that do not meet current standards and lack adequate public
lighting. This office has no plans in place or funding at its disposal to consider these works
at this time.
