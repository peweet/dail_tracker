PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION 
 
1. 
On the proposal of Comhairleoir O. Moran, seconded by Comhairleoir J. Maher, a 
vote was called for on whether or not to exercise An Chomhairle’s right to nominate 
a candidate for the Presidential Election, where there appeared as follows:- 
 
FOR: Comhairleoirí J. Maher, T. Tynan, O. Moran, N. O’Flynn, H. Kamegni, P. 
Horgan, D. Boyle, N. O’Connor, A. Deasy, C. O’Connor. (10) 
 
AGAINST: Comhairleoirí J. Kavanagh, M. McDonnell, D. Boylan, J. Sheehan, M. 
Gould, K. Collins, T. Shannon, M.R. Desmond, D. Cahill, S. Martin, F. Kerins, C. 
Kelleher, J. Lynch, T. Coleman, G. O’Brien. (15) 
 
ABSTAIN: Comhairleoirí B. McCarthy, F. Dennehy. (2) 
 
As those voting against were greater than those voting for, An Chomhairle declared 
the vote failed and no candidate nominated. 
 
This concluded the business of the meeting
