# Research Brief for Claude — Additional Public-Record Intelligence Sources for Dáil Tracker

**Prepared:** 2026-06-05  
**Purpose:** Provide a curated list of Irish public-data sources worth researching for Dáil Tracker’s due-diligence, public-money, housing, procurement, infrastructure, and governance intelligence layers.

This brief intentionally avoids architecture recommendations, implementation phases, and product planning. It is a source-discovery brief.

---

## 1. Board minutes, meeting minutes, agendas, and decision records

| Source | Public data available | Why it is useful |
|---|---|---|
| Iarnród Éireann / Irish Rail — FOI request records | FOI logs include requests for Irish Rail board minutes, executive group minutes, DART expansion records, Railway Investigation Unit correspondence, and governance records. https://www.irishrail.ie/en-ie/about-us/company-information/freedom-of-information/request-records | Strong signal for records that exist but may not be proactively published. |
| Iarnród Éireann — Board of Directors page | Confirms board papers/minutes exist and are managed by the Company Secretary, although not generally published as a minutes archive. https://www.irishrail.ie/en-ie/about-us/company-information/iarnrod-eireann-board-of-directors | Useful for FOI/AIE targeting and governance mapping. |
| Iarnród Éireann — Annual Reports | Annual reports from 2013–2024, company governance, financials, performance, board and audit context. https://www.irishrail.ie/en-ie/about-us/company-information/iarnrod-eireann-annual-reports | Core semi-state due-diligence source. |
| Iarnród Éireann — Protected Disclosure annual reports | Protected-disclosure annual reporting by year. https://www.irishrail.ie/en-ie/about-us/company-information/protected-disclosure-policy-and-procedures | Compliance/governance signal. |
| Iarnród Éireann — AIE request records | Environmental-information request records. https://www.irishrail.ie/en-ie/about-us/company-information/freedom-of-information/acccess-information-environment/requests-records | Useful for infrastructure, environmental, planning, rail projects. |
| Transport Infrastructure Ireland — Board minutes | TII states ordinary board minutes are published quarterly, with occasional redactions; PDFs exist for recent meetings. https://www.tii.ie/en/tii-library/reports-accounts/ | Excellent infrastructure-decision source. |
| National Transport Authority — Board minutes | Board page lists minutes across many years; PDFs available for monthly meetings. https://www.nationaltransport.ie/about-us/board/ | Transport funding, public transport programmes, bus/rail strategy, major project oversight. |
| HSE — Board minutes | HSE publishes board meeting minutes and recent board publications. https://about.hse.ie/publications/?type=Board+meeting+minutes | Health procurement, capital, governance, performance, risk. |
| HSE — Board committee minutes | HSE publishes committee minutes including Performance Committee, Audit & Risk Committee, and Strategy and Reform Committee. https://about.hse.ie/publications/?type=Committees+of+the+board+meeting+minutes | Very strong risk/performance/audit source. |
| Land Development Agency — Board meeting minutes | LDA publications include Board Meeting Minutes; compliance page describes them as formal records of discussions, decisions, actions, performance, financial matters, and strategic initiatives. https://lda.ie/about-us/publications | High-value housing/land/development source. |
| Land Development Agency — FOI decision log | FOI log includes requests for board minutes, board presentations, reports to the board, Project Tosaigh records, action trackers, and risk registers. https://lda.ie/foi-decision-log-2025 | Excellent due-diligence signal around what records exist. |
| Land Development Agency — Board-minute PDFs | Individual board-minute PDFs include decisions such as financial-statement approval and project/governance items. https://lda.ie/uploads/documents/bm-29-08-2025.pdf | Direct source for decisions and governance chronology. |
| Strategic Banking Corporation of Ireland — Board minutes | SBCI publishes board-minute PDFs. Examples include protected disclosures, compliance, AML, data protection, risk, annual reports, schemes, and audit/risk committee reporting. https://sbci.gov.ie/uploads/general/SBCI-Board-Minutes-25-February-2025.pdf | Strong finance/state-support/business-scheme source. |
| Maritime Area Regulatory Authority — Board minutes | MARA board page states board minutes are available; PDFs include protected-disclosure policy, finance procedures, ARC reports, strategic-plan progress. https://www.maritimeregulator.ie/about/who-we-are/the-board/ | Offshore renewables, maritime consents, infrastructure, enforcement governance. |
| MARA — Stakeholder Consultative Forum minutes | Forum page states minutes are published and gives resource links; forum covers MARA stakeholder engagement. https://www.maritimeregulator.ie/about/engagement/stakeholder-consultative-forum/ | Useful for offshore/infrastructure stakeholder intelligence. |
| Higher Education Authority — Board meeting reports | HEA publishes board meeting reports/minutes; examples include corporate risk register, protected disclosures, FOI, annual reports, internal audit, governance. https://hea.ie/assets/uploads/2017/04/Report-of-the-Meeting-of-the-HEA-Board-1st-July-2025-Final.pdf | Useful for institutional funding, governance, risk, higher-education capital. |
| Quality and Qualifications Ireland — Board meeting notes | QQI board notes include procurement internal-audit review, protected disclosures, governance, risk, and committee appointments. https://www.qqi.ie/sites/default/files/2022-07/QQI-N60%20Note%20of%20the%20February%202022%20Meeting.pdf | Good example of public-body board minutes with procurement/risk content. |
| Dublin City Council — ModernGov meetings | Meeting pages, agendas, reports, and minutes. https://dublin.moderngov.co.uk/ieListMeetings.aspx | Local decisions, motions, land, housing, finance, planning, area committees. |
| Dublin City Council — Public-i webcasts | Archived meeting webcasts with agenda-item timestamps and linked resources. https://dublincity.public-i.tv/core/portal/home | Evidence trail from agenda item to video discussion. |
| South Dublin County Council — Meetings | Agendas, minutes, and webcasts. https://www.sdcc.ie/en/services/our-council/council-meetings/ | Council-level decisions and local policy context. |
| South Dublin County Council — Meeting portal | Searchable meeting list and agenda/minutes portal. https://meetings.southdublin.ie/ | Structured local-authority meeting data. |
| Wicklow County Council — Meetings | Minutes, agendas, transcripts, calendar, and live streaming. https://www.wicklow.ie/Living/Your-Council/Council-Meetings | Transcripts make it unusually valuable. |
| Wexford County Council — Agendas and minutes | County council agendas, statutory budget meetings, special planning meetings, and special housing meetings. https://www.wexfordcoco.ie/council-and-democracy/council-plans-publications-reports-and-minutes/county-council-meetings-agendas-and-minutes | Explicit housing/planning/budget meeting types. |
| Donegal County Council — Council meetings | Agendas and minutes including annual budget meetings and special meetings. https://www.donegalcoco.ie/en/services/corporate/council-meetings | Useful for budget-meeting and minutes extraction. |
| Fingal County Council — Meetings | Meeting minutes/agendas and modern.gov app reference. https://www.fingal.ie/council-meetings | Likely structured council meeting source. |
| Cork County Council — Full council meetings | Agendas and minutes for full council and other meetings. https://www.corkcoco.ie/en/council/elected-members-meetings-agendas-and-minutes/meetings-agendas-and-minutes/full-council-meetings | County-level meeting records. |
| Cork City Council — Full council meetings | Meeting schedule, attendance, minutes, and agendas. https://www.corkcity.ie/en/council-services/councillors-and-democracy/meetings-of-the-city-council/full-council-meetings/ | Useful for attendance plus decision chronology. |
| Sligo County Council — Agendas | Agenda pages for ordinary meetings. https://www.sligococo.ie/YourCouncil/CountyCouncil/Agendas/ | Less structured but crawlable. |

## 8. Research questions for Claude

Use the source list above to answer:

1. Which sources expose structured HTML, CSV, XLSX, ArcGIS, API, RSS, PDF, or DOCX data?
2. Which sources publish meeting minutes or board minutes as regular archives?
3. Which sources publish only FOI logs indicating records exist but do not proactively publish the records?
4. Which sources have quarterly or monthly updates?
5. Which sources contain procurement, contract, budget, spend, grant, investment, or delivery data?
6. Which sources contain planning, land, housing, infrastructure, or development signals?
7. Which sources are high-value but privacy-sensitive?
8. Which sources have stable URLs or predictable archive structures?
9. Which source families overlap with existing Dáil Tracker domains?
10. Which sources should be manually reviewed first for due-diligence value?
