Uisce Éireann/Formerly Irish Water

All Boards – Department of Housing, Local Government and Heritage – Uisce Éireann

Legal basis: Section 6A of the Water Services Act 2013 (as amended).
Maximum Number of Positions: 10
Gender Balance Numbers: Female (5), Male (5)
Gender Balance Percentage: Female (50%), Male (50%)
Notes: Under the Act, the Board shall consist of at least 5 and not more than 10 directors (including its chairperson and the chief executive officer); the Chief Executive Officer is an ex officio Director and one of the persons appointed is nominated by the Irish Congress of Trade Unions (ICTU). Uisce Éireann was previously known as Irish Water.
