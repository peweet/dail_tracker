# Required Assessments / Triggers — Wicklow County Council

**Plan:** Wicklow County Development Plan 2021-2027 — Appendix 1: Development & Design Standards
**Fetch date:** 2026-06-13 · Source: `raw/dm_standards.pdf` (two-column PDF)

Triggers referenced in Appendix 1 (assessment procedures are also set out in Volume 1 of the plan):

- **Flood Risk Assessment (FRA)** — §1.5. FRA required with all development in areas identified as having a flood risk, in accordance with *The Planning System and Flood Risk Management Guidelines 2009* (the **Justification Test** basis for development in flood zones).
- **Architectural Heritage / Protected Structures** — §9.2. Works affecting protected structures / places of worship assessed under the *Architectural Heritage Protection Guidelines for Planning Authorities* (AHIA expectation).
- **Nature & Biodiversity** — §1.3. Proposals with potential to affect natural features/water quality require demonstration of no deterioration; Natura/Appropriate Assessment screening applies (statutory, handled in Volume 1).
- **Environmental / EPA licensing** — references to the Waste Management Act 1996 and Industrial Emissions licensing where relevant; environmental impact to be minimised.
- **Traffic & Transport** — sightline/road standards reference the *Traffic & Transport Assessment Guidelines 2014* (TTA above threshold).

> Note: statutory **Appropriate Assessment (Natura 2000)** and **EIA/EIAR** screening/determination are governed by Volume 1 and national regulations, not enumerated as a numeric trigger table in Appendix 1. Recorded here as referenced, without inference on thresholds.
