7. PPN Report:  
MD updated the committee with the PPN report.  Clarified to the committee that the role of 
the PPN is to support community groups to represent their respective pillars on council 
committees.  
The Chairperson clarified the following about the PPN Secretariat: 
It is governed by the DRCD and is independent of the LCDC/CCC.

a. LECP Update: LECP 2024-2029  
‘Action Tracker’

No report presented to this meeting at this time.

MD advised the committee that €142k in funding has been received by Sláintecare.  
Projects are being implemented on an ongoing basis. 
 
d. Healthy Ireland Update:  
Janet O’Connor, Healthy Ireland Co-Ordinator presented to the committee. Ms. 
O’Connor outlined her role as a Healthy Ireland Co-Ordinator. She gave an overview 
of the 2025 activity workplan and budgeting profile. She also gave details of reporting 
structures and timelines.  
The Chairperson congratulated and thanked Janet for her presentation.
