## Apartment design SPPRs (16.8.11)

- Dual aspect minimum: 33% (central/accessible urban), 50% objective (suburban/intermediate); flexibility for refurbishment/infill <0.25 ha
- Ground-level floor-to-ceiling height: 2.7 m minimum
- Maximum 12 apartments per floor per stair/lift core

## Public Open Space — Residential (16.8.7)

- Greenfield sites: **15% minimum** of total site area
- Large infill/brownfield: 10% minimum
- Institutional lands: 20% minimum
- Minimum usable dimension: 10 m

## Car Parking Standards (16.10.11, Table 16.7)

**Residential**
- Dwelling house: 2 spaces/unit (max)
- Apartment/flat: 1.5 spaces/unit (max)
- Hotel/B&B/guesthouse: 1 space/bedroom
- Hostel: 1 space/bedroom or 1 per 10-bed dormitory
- Student accommodation: Planning Authority discretion

**Retail**
- Food retail: 1 per 20 m² GFA
- Non-food retail: 6 per 100 m² GFA
- Retail warehousing: 1 per 35 m² net retail floor
- Cash and carry: 1 per 100 m²
- Service station retail: 1 per fuel pump + 1 per 10 m² net floor

**Commercial**
- Banks/financial: 1 per 20 m² GFA
- Bars/lounges: 1 per 10 m²
- Restaurants: 1 per 20 m² GFA
- Café: 1 per 10 m² dining area
- Takeaway: 1 per 18 m² GFA
- Car showrooms: 1 per 50 m² GFA
- Cinema: 1 per 10 seats
- Conference centre: 1 per 25 m²
- Warehousing: 1 per 100 m²

**Employment**
- Offices (town centre): 1 per 25 m² GFA
- Offices (business park): 1 per 20 m² GFA
- Industry: 1 per 60 m² GFA

**Health/Education**
- Hospital: 1.5 per bed
- Clinics: 3 spaces/consulting room + staff
- Nursing home: 1 per 2 bedrooms + staff
- Primary school: 1 per classroom + staff/visitor
- Secondary school: 2 per classroom + staff/visitor
- Third level: 0.5 per 1 student + visitor/staff
- Childcare: 1 per staff member + 1 per 4 children

**Community**
- Leisure centre: 1 per 50 m² GFA
- Gym: 1 per 10 m²
- Swimming pool: 5 per 100 m²
- Community centre: 1 per 10 m²
- Sports grounds: 20 per pitch / 2 per court
- Library/museum/gallery: 3 per 100 m² GFA
- Golf course: 4 per hole
- Pitch and putt: 2 per hole
- Golf driving range: 1 per bay + 3 spaces

**Accessible parking:** 5% minimum where 10+ spaces required.

## Bicycle / Cycle Parking (16.10.11, Table 16.7)

**Residential**
- Dwelling house: 1 space/unit (min)
- Apartment/flat: 1 per bedroom/studio
- Hotel/B&B: 1 per 20 beds
- Hostel: 1 per 10 beds
- Student accommodation: 1 per 3 beds

**Retail**
- Food retail: 1 per 75 m²
- Non-food retail: 1 per 100 m²
- Retail warehousing: 1 per 500 m²
- Cash and carry: 1 per 150 m²
- Service stations: 1 per 50 m²
- Cinema: 1 per 20 seats

**Commercial**
- Banks: 1 per 50 m²
- Bars: 1 per 75 m²
- Restaurants: 1 per 75 m²
- Café: 1 per 50 m²
- Conference centre: 1 per 20 seats

**Employment**
- Offices (town centre): 1 per 5 employees
- Offices (other): 1 per 10 employees
- Industry: 1 per 500 m²

**Health/Education**
- Hospital: 1 per 20 beds
- Clinics: 1 per consulting room
- Nursing home: 1 per 5 bedrooms
- Primary school: 1 per 5 students
- Secondary school: 1 per 4 students
- Third level: 1 per 3 students
- Childcare: 1 per 5 staff on duty

**Community**
- Leisure centre / gym / swimming pool / community centre: 1 per 10 m²
- Sports grounds: 5 per field / 1 per court
- Library/museum/gallery: 1 per 50 m²
