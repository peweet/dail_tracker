6. For Information 
 
(a.) LCDC Local Enhancement Programme 2023/2024                                                                       
€284,887 available for distribution. 
196 projects applications received, amounting to over €1 million of an ask from groups 
around the city.   
 
(b.) The Community Recognition (CRF) Fund 2024 (City of Welcomes)  
M.D. reported to the LCDC members that 4 projects in window 3 have now been 
proposed to the Department of Rural and Community Development. Cork City Council 
await their response.  
The CRF/City of Welcome is funding €1.2 Million across the city in 2024.  
 
(c.) Cork City Council Community Development Grants  
Closing date has been extended to Wednesday 12th March 2025.  
Total of €220,000 available for distribution. €160,000.00 in Capital funding and 
€60,000.00 available in Small Scale. 
 
 
(d.) Cork City Council Sports Grants  
Monday 3rd March 2025 will see the launch of Cork City’s Sports Grants. There will be 
€500,000.00 available to sporting communities across the city for Capital projects.

10. SICAP and ECP Report: LCDC Review 
The SICAP Sub-Group having extensively reviewed the Annual Performance 2024 of SICAP 
and the Annual Plan for 2025 recommended to the full LCDC members to adopt and approve 
the SICAP 2024 End of Year report and the SICAP Annual Plan 2025. It also recommended 
that the SICAP Sub-Group would work closely with Cork City Partnership to maximise the 
spend of all budgets. 
 
Proposed by: 
Denis J. Fitzpatrick 
Seconded by: Tony Fitzgerald

to CCP.
