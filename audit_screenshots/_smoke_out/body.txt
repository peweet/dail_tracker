Who the State puts into public office: state-board and agency members, judicial appointments, and the special advisers who serve in ministers' offices. Browse the full record since 2016.

Formal acts of the President and Government (judicial appointments, senior board appointments) are recorded in Irish, the first official language under Article 8. Ministerial appointments are issued in English. The pattern is preserved on every card, with structured English summaries built from curated translations of the constitutional templates.
