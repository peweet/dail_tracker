## Residential Density
- Planning/zoning assumption used throughout the plan: **35 units per hectare**.
- Caravan/camping parks: maximum **50 caravans per hectare (20 per acre)**.

## Public / Communal Open Space
- Greenfield sites: minimum **15% of the overall site area** as public open space.
- (Infill / certain sites): minimum **10% of the overall site area** reserved as public amenity.
- Sites with substantial open lands: minimum **20% of the overall site area**.

## Car Parking (Chapter 16 Technical Standards — selected uses)
- Loading/parking bay dimensions (Table 16.7): **car parking bay 5 m × 2.5 m**.
- Hostels: **1 space per 5 beds** (cycle 1 per 20 beds); Hospitals: **1 per doctor/consultant** or **1 per 3 beds** (cycle 1 per 10 staff); Museums: **1 per 100 m²** plus bus parking; Sports hall: **1 per 50 m²** plus bus parking.
- Car-parking areas to be landscaped/tree-planted; financial contributions in lieu of town-centre parking sought per policy.
- *(Residential dwelling parking ratios and separation distances are set elsewhere in the plan / the Rural Siting Guide — not cleanly tabulated in the extracted Chapter 16 text; see `raw/`.)*

## Rural / One-off Housing
- Ribbon-development guard: in general **5 houses on any one side of 250 m of road frontage**.
- One-off rural housing assessed under the rural-housing policies (RH-P-x) and the companion **Rural Location, Siting & Design Guide 2024-2030** (`raw/rural-location-siting-design-guide-2024-2030.pdf`). Catchment-specific controls apply to single-house effluent treatment systems (RH-P-10).
- Sightlines assessed per relevant national vision lines and stopping sight distances.
