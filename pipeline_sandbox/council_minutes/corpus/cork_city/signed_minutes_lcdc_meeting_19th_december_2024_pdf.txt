6. PPN Report:  
K. H. Gave the LCDC an updated report on the activities of the PPN.  
D. F. raised the issue of the cost involved for voluntary bodies when running programs 
introduced by the Government and suggested the LCDC should raise this issue. 
D. F. spoke to the need for a Disability Officer within Cork City Council.  
R.L advised that there is an action in the LECP which focuses on resourcing services for people 
with disabilities. (HLG 2, Action 2.3.4. Pg 128). 
D.F. requested that the new Chief Executive meet with the PPN to give an overview of the 
reconfiguration of Cork City Council. R.L. advised that this request should come from PPN 
directly to CE.

Below are the revised timelines for submission and review of 2025 Annual Plans 
1.
LCDCs should agree and communicate the 2025 KPI targets to LDCs and submit to
Pobal  Co-ordinator by Friday 1st November 2024. 
1.
LDCs will submit their Annual Plan on IRIS by Friday 13th December 2024.
2.
The LCDC will review the Annual Plan and provide feedback to Pobal on any issues

3. Pobal will carry out a high-level review (to ensure compliance with the Programme
