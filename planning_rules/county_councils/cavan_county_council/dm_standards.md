**Authority:** Cavan County Council
**Plan:** Cavan County Development Plan (incorporating a Local Area Plan for Cavan Town) 2022-2028 (adopted 30 May 2022, effect 11 July 2022)
**Chapter:** Chapter 13 — Development Management (standards at §13.4 / §13.7). Parking standards in Chapter 7 (Table 7.4). Rural siting/sightlines in Chapter 12 (Rural).
**Source:** Written Statement PDF — https://www.cavancoco.ie/file-library/planning/development-plans/development-plan-2022-2028/written-statement/cdp-written-statement.pdf
**Fetch date:** 2026-06-13
**Note:** Sightline visibility 'x'/'y' distances are in a separate document ("Sightline Visibility Requirements for Junctions and Direct Accesses", Appendix 4), NOT in the written statement, and were not captured. Cavan does not set a fixed numeric minimum site area for rural one-off houses — rural houses assessed against rural design/siting criteria (Chapter 12) and EPA wastewater site suitability.

## Building Height (§13.4.2)
No fixed numeric maxima. Compliance with Urban Development and Building Height Guidelines (Dec 2018). Height determined by prevailing context, streetscape, protected structures/ACAs. Building Height Strategy for Cavan Town to be commenced within 2 years.

## Site Coverage (§13.4.3) / Plot Ratio (§13.4.4)
No fixed numeric standard. Assessed on performance-based criteria per NPF NPO 13.

## Private Open Space — new dwelling houses (§13.4.7, POS 02)
- One/Two bedroom: **48-55 m²**
- 3/4/5 bedroom: **60-75 m²**

## Public Open Space (§13.4.8)
Per "Sustainable Residential Development in Urban Areas" Guidelines (DEHLG 2009) — no fixed % stated in chapter.

## Separation / Privacy Distances (§13.4.9, Overlooking & Overshadowing)
- Directly opposing rear first-floor windows (detached/semi-detached/terraced): **22 m minimum** (general)
- Overlooking living-room windows / balconies at upper floors: **35 m** normally required
- Ground-floor window to facing boundary: **not less than 1 m**
- Reductions allowed for single-storey / innovative schemes demonstrating adequate privacy

## Waste / Bin Storage (§13.4 Design & Layout, DL 07)
- Each house: adequate screened storage for **at least 3 wheelie bins**

## EV Charging
- New/replacement rural or urban dwelling: provide **1 EV charging point** (§12, EV objective)
- New car parking: capable of accommodating future EV charging at **10% of total spaces** (CP 09)

## Car Parking Bay Dimensions (Chapter 7, Table 7.4 notes)
- Perpendicular bay: **5.0 m × 2.5 m** minimum
- Parallel bay: **6.0 m × 2.4 m** minimum
- Bicycle stand = shelter with minimum 5 racks per stand

## Rural — Ribbon Development definition (§12.13.2.9)
Ribbon = 5 or more houses on any one side of a given 250 m of road frontage (per DEHLG Sustainable Rural Housing Guidelines). New/extended ribbon development discouraged.

## Rural — Access & Sightlines (§12.13.2.6)
New access points generally subject to sightline visibility standards in Appendix 4 ("Sightline Visibility Requirements for Junctions and Direct Accesses"). Additional access points to national roads with speed limit >60 km/h restricted. (Numeric x/y values are in Appendix 4 — not in written statement, not captured here.)
