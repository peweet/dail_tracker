1. 
RESOLUTION SUBMITTED UNDER SECTION 140 OF THE LOCAL 
GOVERNMENT ACT (2001) 
 
An Chomhairle considered the resolution contained in the notice submitted under 
Section 140 of the Local Government Act (2001):- 
 
‘That Cork City Council rescinds the request for additional funding from central 
government in respect of the contract with BAM for the Cork Event Centre and instead 
secures central government funding for a publicly-owned and operated Cork Event 
Centre.’ 
 
On the proposal of Comhairleoir L. Bogue, seconded by Comhairleoir T. Tynan, a 
vote was called for on the approval of the resolution where there appeared as follows:- 
 
FOR: Comhairleoirí T. Tynan, B. McCarthy, L. Bogue. (3) 
 
AGAINST: Comhairleoirí K. O’Flynn, J. Maher, J. Kavanagh, O. Moran, G. 
Keohane, T. Fitzgerald, M. Nugent, J. Sheehan, K. Collins, D. Boylan, D. Cahill, M.R. 
Desmond, K. McCarthy, M. Finn, D. Boyle, S. Martin, S. O’Callaghan, F. Kerins, F. 
Dennehy, D. Canty, C. Finn, C. Kelleher, G. Kelleher, T. Moloney, O. O’Leary. (25) 
 

ABSTAIN: (0)  
 
As those voting against the resolution were greater than those voting for, An 
tArdmhéara declared the vote and the resolution defeated.  
 
 
 
This concluded the business of the meeting.
