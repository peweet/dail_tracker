Central Bank Commission

All Boards – Department of Finance – Central Bank Commission

Legal basis: Central Bank Reform Act 2010, Schedule 1, Part 1, item 28
Maximum Number of Positions: 12
Gender Balance Numbers: Female (4), Male (5)
Gender Balance Percentage: Female (44%), Male (56%)
Notes: The Commission comprises: The Governor of the Central Bank; the Head of Central Banking; the Head of Financial Regulation; the Secretary General of the Department of Finance, and at least 6, but no more than 8, other members appointed by the Minister.
