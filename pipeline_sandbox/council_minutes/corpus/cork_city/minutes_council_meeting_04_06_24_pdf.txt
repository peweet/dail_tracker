SAFE ROUTES TO SCHOOL – BISHOPSTOWN COMMUNITY SCHOOL – 
SECTION 38 APPROVAL   
 
On the proposal of Comhairleoir G. Kelleher, seconded by Comhairleoir C. Finn, An 
Chomhairle considered and approved the report and recommendation of the Director 
of Services, Roads & Environment Operations, dated 24th May 2024, following public 
consultation, on the proposed Safe Routes to School – Bishopstown Community 
School pursuant to Section 38 of the Road Traffic Act, 1994, as amended by Section 
46 of the Public Transport Regulation Act, 2009. 
 

1.2 
SAFE ROUTES TO SCHOOL – GLANMIRE COMMUNITY COLLEGE – 
SECTION 38 APPROVAL  
 
On the proposal of Comhairleoir J. Maher, seconded by Comhairleoir M. Nugent, An 
Chomhairle considered and approved the report and recommendation of the Director 
of Services, Roads & Environment Operations, dated 20th May 2024, following public 
consultation, on the proposed Safe Routes to School – Glanmire Community College 
pursuant to Section 38 of the Road Traffic Act, 1994, as amended by Section 46 of 
the Public Transport Regulation Act, 2009. 
 
1.3 
CAPITAL ASSISTANCE SCHEME: 5 SMITHGROVE TERRACE, MIDDLE 
GLANMIRE ROAD, CORK - SHANBALLY HOUSING ASSOCIATION CLG 
 
An Chomhairle considered and approved the report of the Director of Services, 
Housing Delivery & Regeneration, dated 22nd May 2024 in relation to a loan facility 
for Shanbally Housing Association CLG in respect of the provision of five units of 
accommodation at 5 Smithgrove Terrace, Middle Glanmire Road, Cork.  
 
On the proposal of Comhairleoir J. Kavanagh, seconded by Comhairleoir T. 
Fitzgerald, An Chomhairle further agreed to adopt the following Resolution:-   
 
“Resolved that, pursuant to the provision of Section 6 of the Housing (Miscellaneous 
Provisions) Act, 1992, a loan facility not exceeding €1,256,490 be granted to 
Shanbally Housing Association CLG, subject to the terms of the Capital Assistance 
Scheme for the provision of 5 one bed units of accommodation at 5 Smithgrove 
Terrace, Middle Glanmire Road, Cork.”

CAPITAL 
ASSISTANCE 
SCHEME: 
20 
GOLDSMITH’S 
AVENUE, 
RICHMOND HILL, CORK - GALTAN CLG 
 
An Chomhairle considered and approved the report of the Director of Services, 
Housing Delivery & Regeneration, dated 22nd May 2024 in relation to a loan facility 
for Galtan CLG in respect of the provision of one unit of accommodation at 20 
Goldsmith’s Avenue, Richmond Hill, Cork. 
 
On the proposal of Comhairleoir J. Sheehan, seconded by Comhairleoir T. Fitzgerald, 
An Chomhairle further agreed to adopt the following Resolution:-  
 
“Resolved that, pursuant to the provision of Section 6 of the Housing (Miscellaneous 
Provisions) Act, 1992, a loan facility not exceeding €225,744.49 be granted to Galtan 
CLG, subject to the terms of the Capital Assistance Scheme for the provision of 1 two 
bed unit of accommodation at 20 Goldsmith’s Avenue, Richmond Hill, Cork.” 
 
1.5 
CAPITAL ASSISTANCE SCHEME: 6, 7, 10, WATERCOURSE MEWS, 
WATERCOURSE ROAD, CORK - FOCUS HOUSING ASSOCIATION CLG 
 
An Chomhairle considered and approved the report of the Director of Services, 
Housing Delivery & Regeneration, dated 22nd May 2024 in relation to a loan facility 
for Focus Housing Association CLG in respect of the provision of three units of 
accommodation at 6, 7, 10, Watercourse Mews, Watercourse Road, Cork.  

 
On the proposal of Comhairleoir D. Boylan, seconded by Comhairleoir T. Moloney, 
An Chomhairle further agreed to adopt the following Resolution:-   
 
“Resolved that, pursuant to the provision of Section 6 of the Housing (Miscellaneous 
Provisions) Act, 1992, a loan facility not exceeding €1,138,500 be granted to Focus 
Housing Association CLG, subject to the terms of the Capital Assistance Scheme for 
the provision of 2 three bed units and 1 four bed unit of accommodation at 6, 7, 10, 
Watercourse Mews, Watercourse Road, Cork.”

ELECTRIC VEHICLE CHARGING STRATEGY

NOISE ACTION PLAN PUBLIC CONSULTATION REPORT 
 
On the proposal of Comhairleoir M. Finn, seconded by Comhairleoir M.R. Desmond,

Operations Directorate, dated the 30th of May 2024 on the Noise Action Plan Public
