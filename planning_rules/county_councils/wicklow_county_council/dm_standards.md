## Residential Density
- **Minimum 50 units per hectare within 500 m** of the centres of the larger towns (Wicklow–Rathnew, Arklow, Bray, Greystones–Delgany).
- **35–50 dwellings/ha** for sites in the medium towns (Baltinglass, Newtownmountkennedy, Aughrim, Carnew, etc.).
- Some settlement tiers: **40+ units/ha** in higher-order locations; **less than 30 dwellings/ha** in smaller/rural settlements.
- Density otherwise determined with regard to the prevailing density of the immediate surroundings.

## Separation / Privacy
- **22 m** above ground level between directly opposing windows (separation may be reduced where opposing windows are avoided / privacy is otherwise secured).
- **0.9 m** minimum separation required in defined circumstances (e.g. between certain boundary conditions — see raw).

## Car Parking (Table 2.x — partial; two-column extraction)
- Dwelling 3–4 bedrooms: **1 space per bedroom**; smaller units **1.5 per (bed/unit)** in apartment contexts.
- Offices: **1.2 per unit** (ground floor), **2 per unit** (above ground floor) — *(per-100 m² basis also given, see raw table)*.
- Restaurant dining: **2–3 per 100 m²** gross; retail/foodstore: **4–10 per 100 m²** gross by type; school (secondary) **0.5 per bed (classroom unit)**.
- Disabled spaces: minimum provision per scheme (see raw).
- Circulation aisles: **6.0 m × 3.0 m**.

## Electric Vehicle / Cycle / Bin
- EV charging: provision of charging points scaled to car-park size (**1 per 5** / **1 per 10** spaces in the cited tables — Table 2.2 Car Charging Point Provision).
- Bicycle parking: Table 2.4 standards (per bedroom + visitor spaces).

## Sightlines & Setbacks (2.1.9–2.1.11)
- Clear sightlines required at entrances; sight distance calculated per road speed/standards.
- Forward sight distance maintained for the **first 6 m** from the public-road carriageway edge, **increasing to 10 m or more** for larger housing developments.
- §2.1.11 sets out setbacks from public roads (see raw for the metric table).

## Open Space / Play
- **0.6 ha casual play spaces (parks)** and **0.2 ha equipped play space (playgrounds)** standards for residential development.

## Rural / One-off Housing
- Assessed under the **Sustainable Rural Housing Guidelines 2005** and the **EPA Code of Practice on Wastewater Treatment and Disposal Systems Serving Single Houses 2010** (on-site wastewater treatment + percolation). No single fixed minimum-site-area figure is tabulated in Appendix 1 (rural housing policy is in Volume 1).
