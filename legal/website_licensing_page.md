# Licensing

Dáil Tracker is **open source and dual-licensed**. Use it free under the
open-source licence, or buy a commercial licence when that suits your business
better.

## Open source — free

The code is published under the **GNU AGPL-3.0**. You're free to use, study,
modify, and self-host it. If you host a **modified** version as a service to
others, the AGPL asks that you publish your modified source under the same
licence. That's it — no fee, no sign-up.

[View the source on GitHub →](https://github.com/peweet/dail_tracker)

## Commercial — for closed/SaaS use

Want to build Dáil Tracker into a **closed-source product**, or offer a hosted
service **without** publishing your modifications? A commercial licence waives
the AGPL's copyleft for you, in exchange for a fee. It's the same software, on
business-friendly terms.

**Typical fee structures:** annual subscription · revenue-share royalty ·
per-deployment. We'll agree the right one with you. Non-profits, journalists, and
researchers — talk to us; you're likely covered free already.

## One thing to know about the data

A licence to the **software** is not a licence to the **data**. Dáil Tracker's
datasets come from public bodies (the Oireachtas, Iris Oifigiúil, the CRO, the
Charities Regulator, SIPO, the CSO, and others) and stay under their own licences.
Commercial licensees comply with those source terms independently. Details in our
[notices](https://github.com/peweet/dail_tracker/blob/main/NOTICE.md).
