**Fetch date:** 2026-06-13

> Triggers as stated in the plan. No inference applied.

## Flood Risk Assessment (Section 8.3)
- Required where lands are liable to flooding or development proposals may give rise to flooding.
- Must follow the Justification Test in the Planning System and Flood Risk Management Guidelines.

## Traffic and Transport Assessment (Section 7.5)
- Traffic Impact Assessment required where traffic generated exceeds 10% of existing traffic level, or 5% where the road is congested.
- Road Safety Audit for all significant developments, including those generating 40+ traffic movements per day.

## Effluent / Site Suitability (Section 2.10)
- Required for unserviced rural areas.
- Assessment carried out in accordance with the EPA Code of Practice, by a suitably qualified person (EPA FETAC certificate).
