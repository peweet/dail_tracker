# Required Assessments / Triggers — Limerick City and County Council

Source: Limerick Development Plan 2022–2028, Chapter 11 — Development Management Standards. Fetched 2026-06-13.
URL: https://mypoint.limerick.ie/en/consultation/draft-limerick-development-plan-2022-2028/chapter/chapter-11-development-management-standards
Raw PDF: https://www.limerick.ie/sites/default/files/media/documents/2021-06/12-chapter-11-development-management-standards.pdf

The chapter references the following assessment triggers:

- **Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA):** referenced within the planning process; thresholds per Schedule 1 of the Planning & Development Regulations 2001.
- **Appropriate Assessment:** European sites / protected areas screening required, especially for rural development and heritage-sensitive locations.
- **Flood Risk:** "Flood Risk Justification Test" for development in flood-prone areas (per Guidelines for Planning Authorities, Nov 2009).
- **Traffic / Transport Assessment:** Retail Impact Assessment (RIA) & Transport Impact Assessment (TIA) for retail (scale/location dependent); Mobility Management Plan for significant trip-generation and enterprise >1,000 m²; School Travel Plan / Mobility Management Plan for school development.
- **Noise Assessment:** Acoustic Design Statement where noise is an issue; commercial/industrial per BS 4142:2014+A1:2019.
- **Heritage & Archaeology:** Design Statement for sensitive sites (medieval streets, protected views, scenic/elevated areas, ACAs); archaeological clearance / curtilage of Protected Structures assessment.
- **Tree / Arboricultural Assessment:** tree survey, retention/removal plan, protection plan and method statement per BS 5837:2012.
- **Sustainability Statements:** triggered at 10+ dwellings, 500 m² retail/commercial/office (urban), or 1,000 m² enterprise/employment.
- **Odour Assessment:** Odour Impact Assessment for takeaway/restaurant facilities.
- **Site Suitability & Percolation Testing:** rural one-off housing per EPA 2021 Code of Practice (site assessment by a registered site assessor); SuDS proposals require percolation/infiltration tests by an engineer (min €2m professional indemnity insurance).
- **Stormwater Audits:** Stage 1, 2 & 3 audits for sites >0.5 ha and SHDs; mandatory for developments to be Taken in Charge.
- **Construction Environmental Management Plan (CEMP):** for industrial/commercial/warehousing >0.5 ha.
