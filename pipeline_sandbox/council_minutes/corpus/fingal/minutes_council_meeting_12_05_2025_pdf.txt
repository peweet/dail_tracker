F/240/25 
   (Agenda Item No 26) 
Draft Strategic Policy Group (SPC) Standing Orders and Fingal County 
Council Standing Orders 
 
The report by the Chief Executive which had been circulated was CONSIDERED.  
A presentation was given to the Members by Mary Egan, Director of Services, 
People, Corporate & Digital Services. 
  
During the course of the discussion, the Mayor B. McDonagh informed the 
Members that an amendment has been received. 
  
The following amendment, in the name of Councillor A. Tormey was proposed by 
Councillor A. Tormey and seconded by Councillor J. Humphreys: 
  
“32B Strategic Policy Committees shall meet quarterly at 4.30pm to conclude at 
6.30pm or at a time agreed by the Committee except for the Housing Strategic 
Policy Committee which starts at 4.00 pm and concludes at 6.00pm. 
  
Amend to: 
32B Strategic Policy Committees shall meet quarterly at 4.30pm to conclude at 
6.30pm or at a time agreed by the Committee except for the Housing Strategic 
Policy Committee which starts at 4.00 pm and concludes at 6.00pm. All 
Strategic Policy Committees shall meet in a hybrid format to facilitate 
online attendance. “ 
  
The Mayor instructed that a roll call vote be taken and on a division the voting 
resulted as follows:  
  
FOR:               25        TWENTY FIVE 
AGAINST:     8          EIGHT 
ABSTAIN:      0          ZERO 
  
FOR:               Councillors Mark Boland, John Burtchaell, Eimear Carbone-Mangan, 
Luke Corkery, Eoghan Dockrell, Jimmy Guerin, Breda Hanaphy, Cathal Haughey,
