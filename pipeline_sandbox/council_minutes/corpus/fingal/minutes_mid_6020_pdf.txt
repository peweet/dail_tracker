b 
Local Property Tax Variation  
 
The attached report by the Chief Executive which had been circulated was 
CONSIDERED.   
  
Mr Oliver Hunt, Head of Finance, gave a presentation to the Members.  
  
The Mayor informed the members two motions were received: 
  
The following motion was submitted and proposed by the Mayor Councillor B. 
McDonagh and seconded by Councillor E. O’Brien: 
  
MOTION TO VARY THE BASIC RATE OF LOCAL PROPERTY TAX IN THE 
ADMINISTRATIVE AREA OF FINGAL COUNTY COUNCIL 
  
“That the County Council determines in pursuance of the provisions of Section 
20 of the Finance (Local Property Tax) Act 2012 (as amended), that the basic rate 
of Local Property Tax stand varied downwards by 7.5 per cent for the period 1st 
November 2024 to 31st October 2025 in respect of relevant residential 
properties situated in the administrative area of Fingal County Council.” 
  
The following motion was submitted and proposed by Councillor A. Graves and 
seconded by Councillor M. Quinn: 
  
“That this Council agrees to the 15% reduction allowable in relation to LPT.” 
  
During discussions, the following motion was submitted and proposed by 
Councillor J. Burtchaell and seconded by Councillor R. Coppinger:  
  
“That this committee agrees an LPT adjustment factor of  minus 15% to reduce 
the burden on ordinary home owners in the context of the cost of living crisis 
which is heavily impacting which families across the county, the proliferation of 
significant management fees in many newer developments resulting in reduced 
operation costs for Fingal & with the median purchase price of a home in Fingal 
now having risen to €410k which will result in increased Revenue LPT charges 
when the next valuation occurs.” 
  
Following further discussions, the Mayor then instructed that a roll call vote be 
taken on the motion Local Property Tax stand varied downwards by 7.5 per cent 
and on a division the voting resulted as follows: 
  

8 
 
FOR:            29      TWENTY NINE 
AGAINST:    10      TEN 
ABSTAIN:     0        ZERO 
  
FOR: Councillors Cathal Boland; Darragh Butler; Eimear Carbone-Mangan; Luke 
Corkery; Kieran Dennison; Eoghan Dockrell; Jimmy Guerin; Cathal Haughey; Joan 
Hopkins; James Humphreys; Corina Johnston; Tom Kitt; Ted Leddy; Grainne 
Maguire; Brian Mc Donagh; Mary McCamley; Paul Mulville; Tony Murphy; Joe

Brendan Ryan; Gerard Sheehan; Siobhan Shovlin; Aoibhinn Tormey; Ellen Troy;

AGAINST: Councillors John Burtchaell; Ruth Coppinger; Angela Donnelly; Tania
