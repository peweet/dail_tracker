## Residential density / building density
- No single fixed dwellings-per-hectare figure is set in Chapter 14. Building Density (14.5.2) promotes "compact growth and consolidation" and supports densities "as expressed in national and regional policies" (NPF, RSES and Section 28 guidelines). Density determined on a site-by-site basis.

## Separation / privacy distances
- **Minimum 22 metres** between directly opposing rear first-floor windows (Objective DMSO23 — Separation Distance), "normally resulting in a minimum rear garden depth of 11 metres."
- Where sufficient alternative private open space (e.g. to the side) is available, the rear garden depth may be reduced — subject to maintenance of privacy.
- The 22 m standard applies to apartments up to three storeys high. In taller blocks a greater separation distance may be prescribed having regard to layout/siting. (14.6.6.3)
- Apartments ground floor adjoining a public area: "privacy strip" of approximately **1.5 m** in depth recommended.

## Private open space (houses) — Objective DMSO27 / DMSO28
| House type | Minimum private open space |
|---|---|
| 3 bedrooms or less | 60 sq.m (behind front building line) |
| 4 or more bedrooms | 75 sq.m (behind front building line) |
| 1–2 bed townhouses (reduced, infill/corner only) | not less than 48 sq.m |
- Narrow strips of open space to the side of houses are excluded from private open space calculations.

## Public open space — Objective DMSO51
- Public open space provision must **exceed 10%** of the development site area (except in exceptional circumstances).
- Minimum public open space provision of **2.5 hectares per 1,000 population** (Objective DMSO51 text begins "2.5 hectares per 1[,000]...").
- Site area for the calculation excludes lands zoned RU, GB, OS or HA.

## Electric Vehicle parking — 14.17.10
- All **multi-unit residential** developments: EV charging points at **20%** of proposed parking spaces; ducting/infrastructure to allow future fit-out at **all** spaces.
- **Non-residential** development: functioning EV charging points at a minimum of **10%** of all spaces; ducting to all other spaces.

## Bicycle parking — Table 14.16 / 14.17.2
- Standards set out in Table 14.16. Categorised short-stay vs long-stay.
- Short-stay bicycle parking should ideally be situated no further than **15 m** from main entry points.
- (Numeric per-use ratios are in Table 14.16 of the source.)

## Building lines / setbacks
- Development not to be carried out in front of established building lines (14.4.9 Building Lines).
- Objective DMSO119: seek appropriate building setbacks along the road network to facilitate future road improvements.

## Plot ratio / site coverage
- Referenced as design-assessment criteria (within ACA / infill assessment — 14.4) rather than fixed numeric thresholds in Chapter 14.

## Refuse / bin storage
- Each dwelling: indicate proposed location and design of bin storage (Objective DMSO31 — Refuse Storage Areas); secure bin storage for existing and proposed dwellings.
- Apartments: communal refuse storage accessible to each stair/lift core, adequately sized (14.7.13 / 14.9.8).

## Other distance standards
- Pumping station buffer zone: minimum **35–50 metres** from the noise/odour-producing part (smaller buffer possible for developments of <15 houses).

## Rural / one-off housing
- Rural housing addressed via demonstrated need (e.g. care requiring rural location); on-site wastewater percolation areas must comply with the EPA Code of Practice (Wastewater Treatment Systems Serving Single Houses). No single fixed minimum site-area figure quoted in Chapter 14 text captured.
