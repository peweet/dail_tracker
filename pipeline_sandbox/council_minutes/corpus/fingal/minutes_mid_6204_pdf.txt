F/509/22 
   (Agenda Item No 1) 
Draft Annual Budget for Financial Year Ending 31st December 2026 
 
The Draft Annual Budget for Financial Year ending 31st December 2026 which 
had been circulated was CONSIDERED. 
  
The Mayor, Councillor T. O’Leary, gave a brief introduction and outlined the 
proposed format for the meeting, whereby, Mr. Oliver Hunt, Head of Finance, 
would give an overall presentation on the Budget. 
  
Each Division would then be examined individually, and Members would be 
afforded an opportunity to pose questions at the end of the presentations.   
  
The Mayor advised that the Members were required by law to consider and note 
each Division, and votes would be taken on the various motions. 
  
The Mayor also advised the Members that he had received five Motions, three of 
which had been ruled out of order.  The other two Motions would be heard at 
the end of the divisional presentations. 
  
 Mr. Oliver Hunt, Head of Finance, thanked his colleagues in Fingal County 
Council and his team in the Finance Department for their work in the 
compilation of this Budget.

BUDGET – MOTION 4 
  
The following Motion was read into the record of the meeting by the Mayor, 
Councillor T. O’Leary.  It was proposed by Councillor K. Dennison, seconded by 
Councillor E. Carbone-Mangan and AGREED: 
  
“That Fingal County Council makes a scheme for the abatement of rates in 
respect of vacant properties in accordance with the provisions of Section 9 of the 
Local Government Rates and Other Matters Act 2019, as amended. 
  
Such a scheme in respect of vacant property will provide for an abatement of 0% 
of rates due to Fingal County Council by a liable person for the financial year 
ending 31 December 2026”
