> Triggers as stated in the Written Statement. No inference applied.

## Flood Risk / Justification Test
- Development in flood zones A and B must pass the development-management Justification Test (per Planning System and Flood Risk Management Guidelines, DEHLG 2009). Rural one-off housing (DM RH 1) must comply with these Guidelines.

## Site Suitability / Wastewater (Rural — DM RH 1)
- Unserviced sites must accommodate on-site wastewater per EPA Code of Practice for Wastewater Treatment Systems for single houses (2009) and the County Laois Groundwater Protection Scheme.
- Safe vehicular access without removing extensive native hedgerow/trees; capacity of area to absorb further development assessed (existing development extent, ribbon/piecemeal development, single landholding).

## Heritage / Vernacular
- Development Management Standards on vernacular structures provided (architectural heritage considerations apply through the plan's heritage chapter).

## Note
The adopted plan distributes Development Management Standards across topic chapters. Explicit assessment triggers captured: Flood Risk Justification Test, on-site wastewater/site-suitability (EPA CoP), and groundwater protection. Appropriate Assessment (Habitats Directive), EIA/EIAR (Schedule 5 thresholds) and Architectural Heritage Impact Assessment / protected-structure procedures are dealt with in the plan's environment and heritage chapters/appendices; treat enumeration of those as partial relative to this DM extract.
