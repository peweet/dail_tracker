# Leitrim County Council — Required Assessments / Assessment Triggers

**Authority:** Leitrim County Council
**Plan:** Leitrim County Development Plan 2023-2029
**Chapter:** Chapter 13 — Development Management Standards (Volume I, §13.2)

**Fetch date:** 2026-06-13

> Triggers enumerated in Chapter 13 (§13.2). No inference applied.

## Appropriate Assessment (13.2.5)
- Under Article 6(3) EU Habitats Directive (1992): Planning Authority must consider/determine nature-conservation implications (incl. in-combination effects) on the Natura 2000 site network before any decision.
- Where applicant's AA Screening concludes AA is required, a Natura Impact Statement must be prepared.
- Approval only where, on best scientific evidence, no significant adverse effects on the integrity of any European site.

## Environmental Impact Assessment (13.2.6)
- Per EIA Directive (2011/92/EU as amended by 2014/52/EU), transposed into Section 5 (Parts 1 & 2) of the Planning and Development Regulations 2001 (as amended).
- Part 1 projects require EIA if threshold met/exceeded (or none set); sub-threshold require screening if not in Part 2 with lower threshold.
- Part 2 projects require EIA if threshold met/exceeded; sub-threshold require screening for EIA.
- EIA process culminates in an Environmental Impact Assessment Report (EIAR) by the applicant.

## Flood Risk Assessment (13.2.7)
- Site-specific FRA required where flooding may be an issue, by a suitably qualified/indemnified professional, per Planning System and Flood Risk Management Guidelines (DEHLG & OPW, 2009).
- Must address climate change, residual risk, sequential approach, and demonstrate no increase in flood risk elsewhere.

## Ecological Impact Assessment (13.2.8)
- EcIA may be required for development likely to significantly impact flora/fauna/wildlife habitats (incl. rare/threatened species); case-by-case; per CIEEM (2019) guidance.

## Other Assessments (13.2.9) — may be required:
- Traffic and Transport Assessment
- Landscape Character Impact Assessment
- Visual Impact Assessment
- Archaeological Assessment
- Architectural Heritage Assessment

## Site Suitability (Rural)
- On-site wastewater per EPA Code of Practice for Domestic Waste Water Treatment Systems (p.e. <10) (2021); percolation/separation distances per EPA Manual; bored-well construction per IGI Guidelines.

## Pre-planning (13.2.1)
- Pre-planning consultation available (Section 247 process referenced in the plan).
