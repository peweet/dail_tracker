1. DRAFT TRAVELLER ACCOMMODATION PLAN (TAP) 2025-2029 
 
An Chomhairle considered the adoption of the Draft Traveller Accommodation Plan (TAP) 
2025-2029. 
 
On the proposal of Comhairleoir J.Kavanagh, seconded by Comhairleoir D. Cahill, a vote 
was called for or against the adoption of the Draft Traveller Accommodation Plan (TAP) 
2025-2029, where there appeared as follows:- 
 
FOR:  
Comhairleoirí J. Maher, M. McDonnell, T. Tynan, O. Moran, T. Fitzgerald, 
D. Boylan, J. Sheehan, M. Gould, K. Collins, B. McCarthy, M.R. Desmond, 
H. Kamegni, P. Horgan, S. Martin, F. Kerins, D. Boyle, N. O’Connor, C. 
Kelleher, J. Lynch, A. Deasy, T. Coleman. (21) 
 
AGAINST: Comhairleoirí J. Kavanagh, N. O’Flynn, D. Cahill. (3) 
 
As those voting in favour of adoption of the Draft Traveller Accommodation Plan (TAP) 
were greater than those voting against, an tArdmhéara declared the Draft Traveller 
Accommodation Plan (TAP) adopted and therefore:- 
 
“Cork City Council hereby resolves to adopt the Draft Traveller Accommodation 
Programme as required under Section 11(d) of the Housing (Traveller Accommodation) 
Act 1998 as advertised including the modifications outlined in the Chief Executive Report.” 
 
 

 
 
 
This concluded the business of the meeting.
