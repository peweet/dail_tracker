# Required Assessments / Triggers — Kilkenny County Council

**Plan:** Kilkenny City and County Development Plan 2021-2027 (Volume 1)
**Fetch date:** 2026-06-13 · Source: `raw/dm_standards.pdf`

- **Appropriate Assessment (AA)** — §1.3. Any plan or project not directly connected with the management of a **Natura 2000 site** must be **screened**; if a likely significant effect cannot be excluded, AA (based on best scientific knowledge) is required, per **Articles 6(3)/6(4) of the Habitats Directive** and **Section 177 of the Planning & Development Act 2000 (as amended)**. The Council is the competent authority.
- **Flood Risk Assessment** — a **Strategic Flood Risk Assessment** was carried out for the county; zoning that failed the **Plan-Making Justification Test** was rezoned. Site-specific FRA applies per the 2009 Guidelines.
- **Architectural Heritage / Protected Structures** — §9.3 + Chapter on built heritage; protection of protected structures, their **curtilage and attendant grounds**, and **Architectural Conservation Areas** (§9.3.3). AHIA expectation for works affecting them.
- **Traffic & Transport Assessment** — Policy 11 (Traffic and Transport Assessment) requires a TTA above threshold.
- **EIA / EIAR** — governed by national regulations (Schedule 5 thresholds); applied where development exceeds thresholds. Not enumerated as a numeric table in Chapter 13.

> Recorded as referenced in the plan; no inference applied to thresholds.
