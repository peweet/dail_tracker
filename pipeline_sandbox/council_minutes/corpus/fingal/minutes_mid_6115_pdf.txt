F/541/24 
   (Agenda Item No ) 
SUSPENSION OF STANDING ORDERS 
 
The Mayor, Councillor B. McDonagh suspended standing orders, due to a 
technical issue at the meeting. 
  
F/542/24 
   (Agenda Item No 1) 
Filling of casual vacancy on Fingal County Council following the election of 
Councillor Ruth Coppinger to Dáil Éireann 
 
It was AGREED to DEFER this item. 
  
F/543/24 
   (Agenda Item No 2) 
Filling of casual vacancy on Fingal County Council following the election of 
Councillor Ann Graves to Dáil Éireann 
 
The report by the Chief Executive, setting out the required procedures, which 
had been circulated was READ. 
  
The nomination of John Smyth to fill the casual vacancy was proposed by 
Councillor A. Donnelly and seconded by Councillor M. Quinn.  
  
In accordance with Section 19 of the Local Government Act 2001, as amended, 
John Smyth was co-opted as a Member of Fingal County Council. 
  
  
  
F/544/24 
   (Agenda Item No 3) 
Filling of casual vacancy on Fingal County Council following the election of 
Councillor Robert O'Donoghue to Dáil Éireann 
 
The report by the Chief Executive, setting out the required procedures, which 
had been circulated was READ. 
  
The nomination of Kevin Humphreys to fill the casual vacancy was proposed by 
Councillor J. Walsh and seconded by Councillor M. McCamley.  
  
In accordance with Section 19 of the Local Government Act 2001, as amended, 
Kevin Humphreys was co-opted as a Member of Fingal County Council.
