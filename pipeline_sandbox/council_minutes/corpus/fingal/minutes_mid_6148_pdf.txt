F/528/25 
   (Agenda Item No 17) 
Draft Development Contribution Scheme 2026-2030 
 
The report by the Chief Executive which had been circulated was CONSIDERED.  
A presentation was given to the Members by Brian Murray, Senior Executive 
Officer, Planning & Strategic Infrastructure Department. 
  
During the course of the discussion, the Mayor informed the members a 
number of motions were submitted.   
  
The following motion, was proposed by Councillor D. Healy and seconded by 
Councillor B. McDonagh: 
  
“That the Scheme be amended to provide a rate of €120.50 per square meter for 
both residential and non-residential development.” 
  
SUSPENSION OF STANDING ORDERS 
It was AGREED to take a 3minute recess. 
  
The Mayor then instructed that a roll call vote be taken and on a division the 
voting resulted as follows:

John Smyth, Ellen Troy, John Walsh. 
  
AGAINST: Councillors Cathal Boland, Darragh Butler, Eimear Carbone-Mangan,

Darren Jack Kelly, Tom Kitt, Ted Leddy, Grainne Maguire, Tony Murphy, Joe 
Newman, Tom O'Leary, JK Onwumereh, Siobhan Shovlin, Aoibhinn Tormey. 
  
ABSTAIN: Councillor Patrick Quinlan. 
  
The Mayor then declared the motion FELL.

Councillor J. Humphreys: 
  
“That the Scheme be amended to remove all exemptions for car parking.”
