Membership of State Boards

In this section of the site you can find a complete list of State Boards, by Department. This shows the range of potential opportunities that exist to serve on a State Board. For each member of a Board information is provided on their name, date appointed, position type and the basis of the appointment. We’ve also provided information on the legal basis of the Board where appropriate.

Every effort is being made to ensure that the information contained here is complete and up-to-date. To assist us in this please send any information on new appointments or anything else we’ve missed to stateboards@per.gov.ie.
