## Site coverage
- CI zone: maximum **0.80 (80%)**
- I zone: maximum **0.80 (80%)**

## Open space

| Type | Standard |
|---|---|
| Communal open space (residential) | 15% of gross site area (min. 10% public open space on small infill) |
| Private open space (per unit) | min. 50% of gross floor area per unit (excl. car spaces) |
| City Centre residential open space | equivalent to 30% of residential GFA, or per 2020 Apartment Standards |
| CI zone open space | 5% of total site area (+50% of residential GFA if residential included) |
| I zone open space | 15% of total site area (2+ industrial units) |

Recreation provision in residential schemes scales with unit count (0–10, 11–20, 21–50, 51–100, 100+ units).

## Separation / privacy distances
- Minimum **11 m** between residential units and private open space / development-potential land (above ground floor); greater for >2 storeys.
- Minimum **1.5 m** between side gables and boundaries.

## Car parking — residential (options)
- Outer Suburbs: e.g. 2 on-site + 1 visitor per 3 dwellings; or 1 on-site + 1 visitor per dwelling; 3 spaces for dwellings >200 m².
- Established Suburbs: 1 on-site + 1 visitor per 3 dwellings; or 1 grouped per dwelling.
- Inner Residential Areas: maximum 1 space per dwelling.
- City Centre: minimized/eliminated for larger apartment schemes; max 1 per dwelling for smaller.

## Cycle parking (Table 11.3, residential)
- Apartments/houses: short-stay 1 per 5 units (min 2); long-stay 1 per unit (min 2).
- Apartments: 1 storage space per bedroom (studio min 1); visitor 1 per 2 units.
- Commercial: min 1 stand per 20 car spaces; each stand min 5 bicycles.

## Rural one-off housing (Zoned A — Agricultural)
- Dwelling sites **shall not normally be less than 0.2 hectares**.
- Access shall not be located directly onto national or regional routes.
- On high ground, dwellings generally single storey, set into the landscape.

## Building setbacks / sightlines (fuel filling stations)
- Access not within **35 m** of a road junction.
- Frontage (primary/secondary/regional road): minimum **20 m** length.
- Pumps/installations: minimum **5 m** setback from road edge.

## EV charging (per EU Energy Performance Regulations 2021)
- New buildings/major renovations (>10 spaces): recharging points + ducting for min 1 per 5 spaces.
- Existing non-dwelling buildings (>20 spaces): min 1 recharging point by 1 Jan 2025.
- All developments: 10% of communal and private spaces adapted for EV chargers.

## Refuse / bin storage
- Houses: storage for three wheeled bins per unit.
- Apartments: 3×240-litre bins per pair, or 3×1,100-litre bins per 10-apartment block.

## Roads / streets / parking dimensions
- Footpaths: 2.5 m (arterial/link low-mod), 3.0 m (mod-high), 4.0 m (high), 1.8 m min (local).
- Carriageway: local 5.0–5.5 m; shared surfaces min 4.8 m (~6 m max).
- Front-garden parking space: 2.5 m × 5 m; vehicular entrance ≤3 m wide.
- Design speed local streets: 10–30 km/h (30 km/h max per DMURS).

## Childcare outdoor play area
- Full day care: 55% of gross floor area (minimum).
